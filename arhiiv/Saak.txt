**Tänane WOTD**
Saak - Nimisõna/Noun
*saak, saagi, saaki; saagid, saakide, saake/saakisid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** see, mida põllul või aias kasvatatud kultuurtaimedelt saadakse (nt puuviljad, mugulad, seemned),see, mis on saadud kindla tegevuse tulemusena
🇪🇪 **2.** see, mida loodusest toiduks korjatakse, kütitakse või püütakse
🇪🇪 **3.** see, mis saadakse kellelegi kallale tungides, kelleltki midagi ära võttes (nt varastades, sõjasaagina)
🇪🇪 **4.** mingis tegevuses saavutatud tulemus
🇬🇧 **1.** harvest, that which is obtained from cultivated plants grown in the field or garden (e.g. fruits, tubers, seeds), that which is obtained as a result of a specific activity
🇬🇧 **2.** that which is gathered, hunted, or caught from nature for food
🇬🇧 **3.** that which is obtained by attacking someone, by taking something from someone (e.g. by stealing, as spoils of war)
🇬🇧 **4.** a result achieved in an activity

**Näited/Examples:**
● Vili oli vägev, saak sügisel suur. ||The fruit was abundant, the harvest was large in the fall.||
● Põllud andsid kesist saaki. ||The fields yielded a meager harvest.||
● Kartulilt on oodata korralikku saaki. ||A good harvest is expected from potatoes.||
● Rikkalik saak. ||A bountiful harvest.||
● Õunasaak. ||Apple harvest.||
● Rukkisaak. ||Rye harvest.||
● Põllult korjatakse saaki sügisel. ||The crop is harvested from the field in autumn.||
● Jahimehe saagiks oli kaks kitse. ||The hunter's prey was two goats.||
● Kalamehed tulid merelt korraliku saagiga. ||Fishermen came from the sea with a good catch.||
● Rüüstajad lahkusid kopsaka saagiga. ||The looters left with a hefty haul.||
