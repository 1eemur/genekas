**Tänane WOTD**
Vanaisa - Nimisõna/Noun
*vanaisa, vanaisa, vanaisa; vanaisad, vanaisade, vanaisasid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** isa või ema isa
🇪🇪 **2.** taevas asuv jumal, taevane jumal
🇬🇧 **1.** grandfather, father or mother's father
🇬🇧 **2.** a god in heaven, a heavenly god

**Näited/Examples:**
● Vanaisa teab lugusid kaugetest maadest. ||Grandpa knows stories from far away lands.||
● Mida küll vanaisa sellises situatsioonis teeks? ||What would Grandpa do in such a situation?||
● Minul – nagu igal normaalsel inimesel – on kaks vanaisa. ||I - like any normal person - have two grandfathers.||
● Lapselaps ütleb, et vanaisa nagunii ei saa aru, mis" äpp" on. ||The grandchild says grandpa doesn't understand what an 'app' is anyway.||
● Nädalavahetusel sõidame Viljandisse vanaisa ja vanaema külastama. ||This weekend we are going to Viljandi to visit grandpa and grandma.||
