Petersell - Nimisõna/Noun
*petersell, peterselli, peterselli; petersellid, petersellide, peterselle/petersellisid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** maitsetaimena kasvatatav lõhestunud lehtedega aiataim,taim, mille lehti kasutatakse maitseainena
🇪🇪 **2.** petersellitaime lehed või lihakas juur maitseainena
🇬🇧 **1.**  parsley, a garden plant with divided leaves grown as a spice, a plant whose leaves are used as a spice
🇬🇧 **2.** parsley leaves or fleshy root as a seasoning

**Näited/Examples:**
● Lisasin supile tilli ja peterselli. ||I added dill and parsley to the soup.||
● Ostsin turult ühe kimbu värsket peterselli. ||I bought a bunch of fresh parsley at the market.||
● Peterselli kasvatatakse peaaegu igas koduaias. ||Parsley is grown in almost every home garden.||
● Maitsestasin supi hakitud tilli ja peterselliga. ||I seasoned the soup with chopped dill and parsley.||
● Mina lisasin toidule ka värsket tükeldatud peterselli. ||I also added fresh chopped parsley to the food.||
