**Tänane WOTD**
Kobama - Tegusõna/Verb
*kobama, kobada; koban, kobasin; kobatud*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.**käega ebakindlalt, ekslevalt, kompides otsima; otsides puudutama 
🇪🇪 **2.** ebakindlalt, sõnu otsides, takerdudes rääkima  
🇬🇧 **1.** to fumble, to grope, to uncertainly and wanderingly search or move by feeling with a hand or foot
🇬🇧 **2.** to speak while faltering, searching for words

**Näited/Examples:**
● Queenie käsi kobas millegi järele, mida ta ei leidnud. ||Quuenie's hand groped for something she didn’t find.||
● Ärevalt ringi vaadates kobas ta jalaga laua all. ||Anxiously, he was feeling around under the table with his foot.||
● Mees kobas taskus ja leidis võtmed. ||The man rummaged in his pocket and found the keys.||
● Ta kobas midagi vabanduseks ja hiilis uksest välja. ||He made some excuse and crept out the door.||
