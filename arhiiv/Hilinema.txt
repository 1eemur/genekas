Hilinema - Tegusõna/Verb
*hilinema, hilineda; hilineb, hilines; hilinenud, hilinetud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** liiga hilja, pärast õiget aega kuhugi jõudma,liiga hilja, pärast õiget aega kuhugi jõudma
🇬🇧 **1.** to be late, to get somewhere after the right time, too late

**Näited/Examples:**
● Riia lennuk hilines ligi tund aega. ||The Riga plane was late for almost an hour.||
● Halva ilma tõttu hilinesid eile hommikul elektrirongid. ||Due to bad weather, electric trains were delayed yesterday morning.||
● Rong hilineb kaks tundi. ||The train is delayed for two hours.||
● Hilinesin loengusse. ||I was late for the lecture.||
● Ta hilines koosolekule. ||He was late for the meeting.||
