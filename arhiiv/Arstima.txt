**Tänane WOTD**
Arstima - Tegusõna/Verb
*arstima, arstida; arstin, arstisin; arstinud, arstitud*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 ravima, ravitsema
🇬🇧 to treat, to heal

**Näited/Examples:**
● Arstid ei oska arstida vähki. ||Doctors don't know how to heal cancer.||
● Doktor lubas sportlase põlve terveks arstida. ||The doctor promised to heal the athlete's knee.||
● Kergem on haiguse eest hoida, kui haigust arstida. ||It is easier to prevent a disease than to cure it.||
● Tammekoore pulbriga arstiti vanast langetõbe. ||Epilepsy was treated with oak bark powder in the old days.||
● Loos esineb uskumine maagilisse loitsu, millega arstitakse põletusi. ||In the story, there is a belief in a magical spell that heals burns.||
