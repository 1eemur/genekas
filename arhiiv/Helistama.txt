Helistama - Tegusõna/Verb
*Helistama, Helistada; Helistab, Helistas; Helistanud, Helistatud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** kellegagi telefoni teel ühendust võtma, telefonikõnet võtma
🇪🇪 **2.** helisema panema; kellahelinaga märku andma 
🇬🇧 **1.** to call, to contact someone by phone, to take a phone call
🇬🇧 **2.** to ring, to give a signal by ringing

**Näited/Examples:**
● Helistasin emale. ||I called my mother.||
● Juhuslikult kohal viibinud taksojuht helistas politseisse. ||A taxi driver who happened to be there called the police.||
● Ma helistasin perearstile ja palusin saatekirja endokrinoloogi juurde. ||I called my family doctor and asked for a referral to an endocrinologist.||
● Jõuluteenistuse lõpus helistati kirikukellasid. ||At the end of the Christmas service, the church bells were rung.||
● Helistasin mitu korda uksekella. ||I rang the doorbell several times.||
