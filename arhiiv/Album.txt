**Tänane WOTD**
Album - Nimisõna/Noun
*album, albumi, albumit; albumid, albumite, albumeid*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** muusikapalade kogumik, mis on salvestatud nt heliplaadile, CD-le 
🇪🇪 **2.** raamat või kaustik, kuhu kogutakse fotosid, marke, mälestussalme vms; virtuaalne fotode vm kogum  
🇪🇪 **3.** kunstiteoste, fotode reproduktsioone sisaldav trükis 
🇬🇧 **1.** album, a collection of pieces of music that have been recorded, e.g. on a record, a CD
🇬🇧 **2.** album, a book or folder in which photographs, stamps, memorabilia, etc. are collected; a virtual collection of photographs or other items
🇬🇧 **3.** printed matter containing reproductions of works of art, photographs

**Näited/Examples:**
● Björki uus album. ||Björk's new album.||
● See Janis lani album on parim. ||This Janis lan album is the best.||
● Kuigi Greenbergile album ei meeldinud, meeldis talle Nicki hääl. ||While Greenberg did not like the album, he did like Nick's voice.||
● Kaheksakümnendatel salvestas ansambel neli albumit. ||In the eighties, the ensemble recorded four albums.||
● Internetis on palju loodusfotode albumeid. ||There are many nature photo albums on the internet.||
● Kunstialbum. ||Art album.||
