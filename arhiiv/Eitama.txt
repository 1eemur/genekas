Eitama - Tegusõna/Verb
*eitama, eitada; eitab, eitas; eitanud, eitatud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** "ei" ütlema,salgama, mitte omaks võtma, alusetuks väitma,ütlema, et mingi väide ei ole tõsi
🇪🇪 **2.** millegi vastu olema, midagi ebaõigeks või mittevajalikuks pidama
🇬🇧 **1.** to say "no", to deny, not to accept, to claim that it is unfounded, to say that a statement is not true
🇬🇧 **2.** to be against something, to consider something improper or unnecessary

**Näited/Examples:**
● Varas eitas oma süüd. ||The thief denied his guilt.||
● Kohtualune eitas oma süüd. ||The defendant denied his guilt.||
● Jüri eitab igasuguseid uuendusi. ||Jüri denies any innovations.||
● Ei saa eitada, et see probleem on mulle tuttav. ||There's no denying that I'm familiar with this problem.||
● Bulgaarlased pidavat eitades pead noogutama ning jaatades pead raputama. ||Bulgarians are said to nod their heads when denying something and shake their heads when agreeing.||
