**Tänane WOTD**
Telefonitsi - Määrsõna/Adverb
*telefonitsi*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 telefoni teel, telefoniga  
🇬🇧 over the phone, by telephone

**Näited/Examples:**
● Leppisime kohtumise telefonitsi kokku. ||We made an appointment by phone.||
● Telefonitsi suhtlen pojaga iga päev. ||I communicate with my son by phone every day.||
● Edastasime info telefonitsi politseile. ||We passed on the information to the police by phone.||
● Reporter kirjeldas olukorda Helsingi lennuväljal telefonitsi. ||A reporter described the situation at the Helsinki aerodrome over the phone.||
● Kui veebipoes esineb tõrge, palume piletid broneerida kas telefonitsi või meili teel. ||If there is a problem with the online shop, please book your tickets either by phone or email.||
