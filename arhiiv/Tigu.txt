**Tänane WOTD**
Tigu - Nimisõna/Noun
*tigu, teo, tigu; teod, tigude, tigusid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** pehme kehaga väike selgrootu loom, kes roomab ja kelle seljal on hrl spiraalne lubiainest koda
🇪🇪 **2.** (osutab võrdlustes kellegi või millegi aeglusele)
🇪🇪 **3.** sisekõrva spiraalne osa, milles paikneb kuulmiselund
🇪🇪 **4.** 1–4-käigulise keermega (silindriline) masinaelement
🇪🇪 **5.** (viiuli osa:) spiraalselt keerduv pea, millega lõpeb viiuli kael
🇬🇧 **1.** snail, a small, soft-bodied, invertebrate animal that crawls and has a spiral chamber on its back
🇬🇧 **2.** (referring to the slowness of someone or something in comparisons)
🇬🇧 **3.** the spiral part of the inner ear, which houses the auditory organ
🇬🇧 **4.** machine element with 1-4-speed thread (cylindrical)
🇬🇧 **5.** (part of a violin) the spirally wound head that terminates the neck of a violin

**Näited/Examples:**
● Söödavad teod. ||Edible snails.||
● Teod ja nälkjad on paljude aiapidajate vihatumad vaenlased. ||Snails and slugs are the most hated enemies of many gardeners.||
● Tänu kolmekihilisele kaitsekilbile on tigu kättesaamatu teda ründavatele krabidele. ||Thanks to the three-layer protective shield, the snail is inaccessible to crabs that attack it.||
● Itaalias, Prantsusmaal, Saksamaal ja Šveitsis peetakse Rooma tigu maitsvaks delikatessiks. ||In Italy, France, Germany and Switzerland, Roman snails are considered a delicious delicacy.||
● Meil on nii kiire, aga tema venib nagu tigu. ||We are in such a hurry, but he drags on like a snail.||
