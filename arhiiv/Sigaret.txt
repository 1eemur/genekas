Sigaret - Nimisõna/Noun
*sigaret, sigareti, sigaretti; sigaretid, sigarettide, sigarette/sigarettisid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.**  tubakatoode, milles peenestatud vääristubaka rull on keeratud paberisse 
🇬🇧 **1.** cigarette, a tobacco product in which a roll of comminuted fine-cut tobacco is wrapped in paper

**Näited/Examples:**
● Suitsetan kaks pakki sigarette päevas. ||I smoke two packs of cigarettes a day.||
● Sigarettide aktsiisi tõstetakse kiires tempos. ||Cigarette excise duty is being increased at a rapid pace.||
● Enamik inimesi ei tea, et sigarettide suitsetamine kahjustab mälu. ||Most people do not know that smoking cigarettes damages memory.||
● Tolliamet konfiskeeris riigipiiril 39 300 pakki sigarette. ||Customs seized 39 300 packets of cigarettes at the border.||
● Vesipiibu suits mõjub kopsudele samamoodi nagu tavaline sigaret. ||Hookah smoke has the same effect on the lungs as a normal cigarette.||
