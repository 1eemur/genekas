Algul - Määrsõna/Adverb & Tagasõna/Postposition 
*algul*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 alguses, hetkel, mil miski peale hakkab 
🇬🇧 at first, at the beginning, the moment when something starts

**Näited/Examples:**
● Möödunud sajandi algul. ||At the beginning of the last century.||
● Asi pole nii hull, kui algul paistis. ||It's not as bad as it seemed at first.||
● Intervjuu ilmus aprilli algul. ||The interview was published in early April.||
● Röövitud raha peideti algul sohu. ||The stolen money was initially hidden in a swamp.||
● Algul töötas ta assistendina, hiljem teadurina. ||At first he worked as an assistant, later as a researcher.||
 
**Sünonüümid/Synonyms:** 
alul
