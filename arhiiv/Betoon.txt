**Tänane WOTD**
Betoon - Nimisõna/Noun
*betoon, betooni, betooni; betoonid, betoonide, betoone/betoonisid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** sideaine (eriti tsemendi), täitematerjali ja vee segu, mis kivistudes muutub kõvaks ning mida kasutatakse ehitusmaterjalina
🇪🇪 **2.** (ehitiste, rajatiste, kinnisvara kohta)
🇬🇧 **1.** contrete, a mixture of binder (especially cement), aggregate and water that hardens when it hardens and is used as a building material
🇬🇧 **2.** (about buildings, facilities, real estate)

**Näited/Examples:**
● Betoonist laepaneelid. ||Concrete ceiling panels.||
● Tuleb oodata, kuni betoon kivistub. ||You have to wait until the concrete hardens.||
● Tsemendi maksumus moodustab betooni hinnast ligi kolmandiku. ||The cost of cement is about a third of the price of concrete.||
● Kulutame tohutuidsummasid betooni peale. ||We spend huge sums on concrete.||
● Liiga palju raha paneme betooni ja põhjendamatult vähe treeneritesse ja noorsportlastesse. ||We put too much money into concrete and unreasonably little into coaches and young athletes.||
