Kodutu - Omadussõna/Adjective
*kodutu, kodutu, kodutut; kodutud, kodutute, kodutuid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** ilma koduta, kindla peavarjuta,ilma koduta
🇪🇪 **2.** inimene, kellel ei ole kodu
🇬🇧 **1.** without a home, without a secure shelter, without a home
🇬🇧 **2.** a person who has no home

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kodutud lapsed. ||Homeless children.||
● Kodutute koerte varjupaik. ||Shelter for homeless dogs.||
● Ta toidab sageli kodutuid koeri ja kasse. ||He often feeds homeless dogs and cats.||
● Kodutute öömaja. ||Homeless shelter.||
● Helde annetus kodututele. ||A generous donation to the homeless.||
● Tänavatel oli näha kodutuid. ||Homeless people were seen on the streets.||
