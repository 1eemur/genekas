Kerjama - Tegusõna/Verb
*kerjama, kerjata; kerjab, kerjas; kerjanud, kerjatud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** teistelt toitu, raha vm paluma või manguma
🇪🇪 **2.** midagi alandlikult, ennast alandades taotlema või nuruma,lunima, manguma
🇬🇧 **1.** to beg, to ask others for food, money, etc.
🇬🇧 **2.** to humbly seek or beg for something, to to appeal for something in a demeaning manner

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kodutu kerjab tänaval. ||Homeless people beg on the streets.||
● Miks haiglad kerjavad raha seadmetele? ||Why are hospitals begging for money for equipment?||
● Poiss kerjab kaupluse ees sente ja suitsu. ||A boy begs for money and cigarettes in front of a store.||
● Laste haiglad kerjavad aparaatide jaoks raha. ||Children's hospitals are begging for money for equipment.||
● Koer kerjas omaniku tähelepanu. ||The dog begged for its owner's attention.||
