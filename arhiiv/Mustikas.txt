**Tänane WOTD**
Mustikas - Nimisõna/Noun
*mustikas, mustika, mustikat; mustikad, mustikate, mustikaid*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** hrl okasmetsas kasvav sinakasmustade söödavate marjadega kääbuspõõsas  
🇪🇪 **1.1** mustikataime mari  
🇪🇪 **1.2** (mustikamarjade korjamise, korjamas käimise kohta)  
🇬🇧 **1.** a hardy dwarf shrub of the heath family, with small, whitish drooping flowers and dark blue edible blueberries
🇬🇧 **1.1** blueberry, the small sweet blue-black edible berry of the blueberry plant
🇬🇧 **1.2** (about picking blueberries, going picking) 

**Näited/Examples:**
● Metsas õitsevad mustikad. ||Blueberries are in bloom in the forest.||
● Peatselt on mustikad korjamisküpsed. ||Soon the blueberries will be ready for picking.||
● Eestis korjati tänavu kokku 2000 tonni mustikaid. ||A total of 2,000 tonnes of blueberries were harvested in Estonia this year.||
● Kalev valge šokolaad õhitud riisi ja mustikaga. ||Kalev white chocolate with puffed rice and blueberries.||
● Läksime metsa mustikale. ||We went blueberry picking in the forest.||
