**Tänane WOTD**
Hunt - Nimisõna/Noun
*hunt, hundi, hunti; hundid, huntide, hunte/huntisid*
*Lühike Sisseütlev: hunti*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** suure koera sarnane enamasti halli värvi kiskja  
🇪🇪 **2.** (kõnekeelne)  tugev koer, keda on kasutatud peamiselt karja ja kodu kaitsmisel ning kes välimuselt meenutab hunti  
🇬🇧 **1.** gray wolf, a predator resembling a large dog, mostly grey in colour
🇬🇧 **2.** (colloquially) a strong dog that has been used mainly to protect livestock and the home and resembles a wolf in appearance

**Näited/Examples:**
● Hunt murdis kitse maha. ||The wolf struck down the goat.||
● Elupaigana eelistab hunt varjulisi metsi ja rabasid. ||The wolf prefers shady woods and bogs for its habitat.||
● Ära mine metsa: seal on hundid ja rebased! ||Don't go into the woods: there are wolves and foxes there!||
● Miks ei ole Lääne-Euroopas enam ammu hunte ja karusid? ||Why are there no wolves and bears in Western Europe any more?||
● Mees torkas tüki leiba noa otsa ja andis hundile. ||A man stuck a piece of bread on the end of a knife and gave it to the wolf.||
