Vallaline - Omadussõna/Adjective & Nimisõna/Noun
*vallaline, vallalise, vallalist; vallalised, vallaliste, vallalisi*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** (omadussõna) abielus mitte olev; 
🇪🇪 **2.** (nimisõna) selline inimene
🇬🇧 **1.** (adjective) not married
🇬🇧 **2.** (noun) such a person, a single person

**Näited/Examples:**
● Marie suri vallalisena. ||Marie died unmarried.||
● Sõbranna on juba jälle vallaline. ||The girlfriend is already single again.||
● Meie hulgas on mitu vallalist meest. ||There are several single men among us.||
● Vallaliste jaoks on õed-vennad nende perekond. ||For single people, siblings are their family.||
● Kursus sobib nii vallalistele kui paaridele. ||The course is suitable for both singles and couples.||
