Viisakalt - Määrsõna/Adverb
*viisakalt*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 hea käitumise tavasid ilmutavalt 
🇬🇧 politely, demonstrating good behavior

**Näited/Examples:**
● Vastus oli viisakalt öeldes eksitav. ||The answer was misleading, to put it politely.||
● Viisakale küsimusele tuleks viisakalt vastata. ||A polite question should be answered politely.||
● Korteris lärmavad külalised saadetakse viisakalt minema. ||Noisy guests in the apartment are politely sent away.||
● Viisakalt naeratades kutsus kuulus tohter mind enda kabinetti. ||Smiling politely, the famous doctor invited me into his office.||
● Ettekandja üritas väga viisakalt seletada, et kahjuks ei pakuta nende restoranis sushit. ||The waiter tried to explain in a very polite way that, unfortunately, their restaurant does not serve sushi.||
 
**Sünonüümid/Synonyms:** 
kombekalt, (obsolete) viisakasti
