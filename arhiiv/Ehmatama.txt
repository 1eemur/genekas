Ehmatama - Tegusõna/Verb
*ehmatama, ehmatada; ehmatab, ehmatas; ehmatanud, ehmatatud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** kedagi järsku, ootamatult hirmutama või ehmuma panema,kedagi järsku, ootamatult hirmutama
🇪🇪 **2.** äkki hirmu tundma, kartma hakkama,järsku hirmu tundma, kartma hakkama
🇬🇧 **1.** to frighten or startle someone suddenly, unexpectedly, to frighten someone suddenly, unexpectedly
🇬🇧 **2.** to suddenly feel afraid, start to be afraid, suddenly feel afraid

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kivideni jõudes ma ehmatasin lendu tosina jagu parte. ||When I reached the rocks, I startled a dozen ducks into flight.||
● Kirjaniku esikteos ehmatas lugejaid keelekasutuse ja vägivallaga. ||The writer's first work startled readers with its use of language and violence.||
● Tuppa sisenenud võõras mees ehmatas naist väga. ||A strange man who entered the room scared the woman very much.||
● Hobune ehmatas püssipaugu peale. ||The horse was startled by the gunshot.||
● Ema ehmatas, kui nägi last katusel. ||The mother was startled when she saw the child on the roof.||
