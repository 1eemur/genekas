**Tänane WOTD**
Erinev - Omadussõna/Adjective
*erinev, erineva, erinevat; erinevad, erinevate, erinevaid*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** mingil viisil teistest, muust eristuv 
🇪🇪 **1.1** mitut liiki või laadi, paljudest eri liikidest 
🇪🇪 **2.** (harv) eraldi olev, omaette esinev, iseseisev 
🇬🇧 **1.** different, distinctive in some way from others
🇬🇧 **1.1** various, of several kinds or types, from many different types
🇬🇧 **2.** distinct, separate, occurring on its own, independent

**Näited/Examples:**
● Need on kaks täiesti erinevat raportit. ||These are two completely different reports.||
● Inimesed on väga erinevad. ||People are very different.||
● Müügil on üheksa erinevat mahla. ||There are nine different juices on sale.||
● Sellega tagab komisjon konkurentsinormide parema leviku erinevates ühistegevusvaldkondades. ||The Commission therefore advocates improving the dissemination of the competition rules amongst the various cooperative sectors.||
● Võisteldi kahes erinevas klassis – profid ja harrastajad. ||They competed in two distinct classes - pros and amateurs.||
