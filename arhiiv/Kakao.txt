**Tänane WOTD**
Kakao - Nimisõna/Noun
*kakao, kakao, kakaod; kakaod, kakaode, kakaosid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** kakaoubadest jahvatatud pulber
🇪🇪 **2.** kakaopulbrist, suhkrust ja piimast valmistatud jook
🇬🇧 **1.** powder ground from cocoa beans
🇬🇧 **2.** a drink made from cocoa powder, sugar and milk

**Näited/Examples:**
● Ostsin paki kakaod. ||I bought a packet of cocoa.||
● Jõin tassitäie sooja kakaod. ||I drank a cup of hot cocoa.||
● Tegin endale tugeva tassi kakaod. ||I made myself a strong cup of cocoa.||
● Soojendasime end mäenõlva mõnusates baarides kuuma tee ja kakaoga. ||We warmed up with hot tea and cocoa in the cosy bars on the hillside.||
● Et eurooplaste maitsele oli kakao liiga kibe, magustati jooki suhkruga. ||As the cocoa was too bitter for European tastes, the drink was sweetened with sugar.||
