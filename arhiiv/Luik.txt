**Tänane WOTD**
Luik - Nimisõna/Noun
*luik, luige, luike; luiged, luikede, luiki/luikesid*
*Lühike Sisseütlev: luike*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 suur pikakaelaline hrl valge sulestikuga veelind  
🇬🇧 swan, a large waterbird with a long flexible neck, short legs, webbed feet, a broad bill, and typically all-white plumage

**Näited/Examples:**
● Järve peal ujuvad valged luiged. ||White swans swim on the lake.||
● Tiigis ujus kaks valget luike. ||Two white swans were swimming in the pond.||
● Tänavu talvitusid seal kolm toonekurge ja luik. ||This year, three storks and a swan wintered there.||
● Pikka aega arvati, et musti luiki ei ole olemas. ||For a long time it was thought that black swans did not exist.||
● Ta istub jõe ääres, vaatab luiki ja sööb kastaneid. ||He sits by the river, watching the swans and eating chestnuts.||
