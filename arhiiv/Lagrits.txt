**Tänane WOTD**
Lagrits - Nimisõna/Noun
*lagrits, lagritsa, lagritsat; lagritsad, lagritsate, lagritsaid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** magus aine, mida saadakse magusjuurena tuntud taime juurtest
🇪🇪 **2.** seda sisaldav(ad) musta värvi maiustus(ed)
🇬🇧 **1.** liquorice, a sweet substance obtained from the roots of a plant known as licorice
🇬🇧 **2.** black candy(s) containing it

**Näited/Examples:**
● Magus lagrits on super hea. ||Sweet liquorice is super good.||
● Lagritsat võin süüa kilode kaupa. ||I can eat licorice by the kilo.||
● Pikka aega on lagritsat kasutatud ka ravimina. ||For a long time, liquorice has also been used as a medicine.||
● Aroomis on marjade kõrval tunda lagritsa ja ürdi noote. ||The aroma has notes of liquorice and herbs alongside the berries.||
● Kurgu kähedust leevendavad taruvaigu pastillid mee ja lagritsaga. ||Propolis lozenges with honey and licorice relieve throat hoarseness.||
