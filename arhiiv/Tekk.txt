Tekk - Nimisõna/Noun
*tekk, teki, tekki; tekid, tekkide, tekke/tekkisid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** hrl magamisel peale võetav kate
🇬🇧 **1.** blanket, usually a cover that can be taken on while sleeping

**Näited/Examples:**
● Vooditekk. ||Bed cover.||
● Villane tekk. ||Wool blanket.||
● Laps ajas teki maha. ||The child threw off the blanket.||
● Palavikus laps higistab teki all. ||A child with a fever sweats under the blanket.||
