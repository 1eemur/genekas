**Tänane WOTD**
Teadma - Tegusõna/Verb
*Teadma, Teada; Teab, Teadis; Teadnud, Teatud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** millegi kohta infot, teadmist või teadmisi omama, millestki teadlik olema, milleski selgusel, kindel olema
🇪🇪 **2.** (da-vormis) esineb ühendites, mis märgivad info ja teadmiste hankimist, edastamist, hoidmist
🇪🇪 **3.** (ära) tundma, tuttav olema
🇪🇪 **4.** oskama; taipama, millegi peale tulema 
🇪🇪 **5.** ebaselgust, ebamäärasust, teadmataolu, kõhklust, kahtlust väljendavana
🇪🇪 **6.** (kõnekeelne) tundetoonilise rõhutamise korral kellegi poole pöördudes, millelegi eriliselt tähelepanu juhtides vms
🇪🇪 **7.** kellegi või millegi mõjule allumist, kellegi kuulda võtmist väljendades
🇬🇧 **1.** to know, to have information, knowledge about something, to be aware of something, to be clear about something
🇬🇧 **2.** (in the -da form) occurs in compounds denoting acquisition, storage of information
🇬🇧 **3.** (to) know, to be familiar with
🇬🇧 **4.** to know; to figure out, to come to a conclusion about something
🇬🇧 **5.** as expressing ambiguity, vagueness, uncertainty, hesitation, doubt
🇬🇧 **6.** (colloquial) in the case of emphatic emphasis, drawing attention to something
🇬🇧 **7.** expressing submission to the influence of someone or something

**Näited/Examples:**
● Maur teab, kust kõrbes vett saab. ||A Moor knows where to get water in the desert.||
● Naine hakkas seda alles aimama, mida arst juba teadis. ||The woman was only beginning to guess what the doctor already knew.||
● Sain võlast teada pangas. ||I found out about the debt at the bank.||
● Anna teada, kus sa ööbid. ||Let me know where you are staying.||
● Teate, ta mees on venelane. ||You know, her husband is Russian.||
