**Tänane WOTD**
Hakkliha - Nimisõna/Noun
*hakkliha, hakkliha, hakkliha; hakklihad, hakklihade, hakklihasid*
*Lühike Sisseütlev: hakklihha*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 peeneks hakitud, läbi hakkmasina aetud liha 
🇬🇧 ground meat, minced meat, finely chopped meat, run through a mincer

**Näited/Examples:**
● Prae sibul koos hakklihaga. ||Fry an onion with the minced meat.||
● Sega hakkliha aeg-ajalt pannil. ||Stir the ground meat in the pan occasionally.||
● Menüüs on täna makaronid hakklihaga. ||On the menu today is pasta with minced meat.||
● Vormi hakklihast pallid ja vajuta lamedaks. ||Shape the minced meat into balls and press them flat.||
● Prae hakkliha pannil kergelt läbi, lisa hakitud sibul ja küüslauk. ||Fry the minced meat lightly in the pan, add the chopped onion and garlic.||
