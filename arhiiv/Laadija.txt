**Tänane WOTD**
Laadija - Nimisõna/Noun
*laadija, laadija, laadijat; laadijad, laadijate, laadijaid*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** inimene, kelle tööks on mingi materjali, kauba vm laadimine 
🇪🇪 **2.** akut laadiv seade 
🇬🇧 **1.** loader, a person whose job it is to load material, goods, etc. 
🇬🇧 **2.** charger, a battery charging device

**Näited/Examples:**
● Isa töötas sadamas laadijana.||The father worked as a loader in the port.||
● Mobiiltelefonide laadijad. ||Mobile phone chargers.||
● Kus on laadija? ||Where is the charger?||
