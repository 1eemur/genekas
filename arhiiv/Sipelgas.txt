Sipelgas - Nimisõna/noun
*sipelgas, sipelga, sipelgat; sipelgad, sipelgate, sipelgaid*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 kiletiivaliste seltsi kuuluv ühiseluline putukas 
🇬🇧 ant, a small insect typically having a sting and living in a complex social colony with one or more breeding queens

**Näited/Examples:**
● Mürgitasin kodus sipelgaid. ||I poisoned ants in my home.||
● Sipelgas on töökuse võrdkuju. ||The ant is the epitome of hard work.||
● Kogu ümbrus kubiseb sipelgatest. ||The whole area is crawling with ants.||
● Kujutlege hetkeks, et te olete sipelgas. ||Imagine for a moment that you are an ant.||
● Pärast punase sipelga hammustust kestab valu ja kipitus pool tundi. ||After a red ant bite, the pain and stinging lasts for half an hour.||
