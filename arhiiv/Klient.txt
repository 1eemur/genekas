**Tänane WOTD**
Klient - Nimisõna/Noun
*klient, kliendi, klienti; kliendid, klientide, kliente/klientisid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** teatud asutuse, ettevõtja või isiku teenuseid kasutav isik
🇪🇪 **2.** sõltlane, patrooni kaitsealune Vana-Roomas
🇬🇧 **1.** customer a person using the services of a certain institution, entrepreneur or person
🇬🇧 **2.** a protégé of a patron in ancient Rome

**Näited/Examples:**
● Pangaklient. ||Bank customer.||
● Rahulolev klient on iga firma unistus. ||A satisfied customer is the dream of every company.||
● Advokaat ajas oma kliendi asju väga osavalt. ||The lawyer managed his client's affairs very skillfully.||
● Selle juuksuri kliendid on tema tööga väga rahul. ||The customers of this hairdresser are very satisfied with his work.||
● Vanade klientidega tuleb tegelda niisama innukalt kui uutega. ||You have to be as enthusiastic dealing with old clients as you are with new ones.||
