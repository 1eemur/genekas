Pahatihti - Määrsõna/Adverb
*pahatihti*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 (kahjuks) üsna tihti 
🇬🇧 (unfortuantely) quite often

**Näited/Examples:**
● Elus juhtub pahatihti õnnetusi. ||Accidents often happen in life.||
● Müsli on maitsev, ent sisaldab pahatihti palju rasva ja suhkrut. ||Muesli is tasty, but often contains a lot of fat and sugar.||
● Pahatihti kipub kodanike kaasamine lõppema kohalike valimistega. ||Citizen involvement often tends to end with local elections.||
● Pahatihti on klienditeenindajad need, kes ärritunud inimeste emotsioonid enda kaela saavad. ||Customer service agents are often the ones who take the blame for upset people's emotions.||
● Pahatihti lõppes tema tööpäev kell neli hommikul, kui ta oli surmväsinud, masendunud ja õnnetu. ||She often ended her workday at four in the morning, when she was exhausted, depressed, and unhappy.||
