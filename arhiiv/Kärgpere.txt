**Tänane WOTD**
Kärgpere - Nimisõna/Noun
*kärgpere, kärgpere, kärgperet; kärgpered, kärgperede, kärgperesid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** pere, kus on sinu-minu-meie lapsed
🇬🇧 **1.** blended family, a family consisting of a couple, the children they have had together, and their children from previous relationships

**Näited/Examples:**
● Tänasel päeval on kärgpere tavaline. ||Today, blended families are common.||
● Mõistet kärgpere kuulsin esimest korda Eestis. ||I first heard about the term "blended family" in Estonia.||
● Siinkohal on kärgpere artiklis vale väljend. ||The term "blended family" is used incorrectly in this article.||
● Koolitusel õpitakse, kuidas kasvatada teismelist üksikvanemana või kärgperes. ||The training teaches how to raise a teenager as a single parent or in a blended family.||
● Üksikvanema elu ja kärgpere elu ei ole üheselt käsitletavad. ||The life of a single parent and the life of a blended family cannot be treated in the same way.||
