**Tänane WOTD**
Esmaabi - Nimisõna/Noun
*esmaabi, esmaabi, esmaabi; -, -, -*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** haavatule, vigastatule või äkki haigestunule (enne arsti saabumist) antav esialgne abi,esimene abi kellelegi, kes on haavatud, vigastatud või järsku haigeks jäänud
🇬🇧 **1.** first aid given to the wounded, injured or suddenly ill (before the doctor arrives), first aid to someone who is wounded, injured or suddenly ill

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kiire esmaabi on väga oluline. ||Rapid first aid is very important.||
● Ta viidi haiglasse, kus tema vigastatud õlale esmaabi anti. ||He was taken to hospital, where his injured shoulder was treated.||
● Arstid andsid ohvrile haiglas esmaabi ja lubasid ta kodusele ravile. ||Doctors provided first aid to the victim at the hospital and allowed him to return home for treatment.||
● Kõik vigastatud lubati pärast esmaabi andmist haiglas kodusele ravile. ||After receiving first aid at the hospital, all of the injured were allowed to return home for treatment.||
● Kiirabitöötajad andsid tulest pääsenule esmaabi ja toimetasid ta seejärel haiglasse. ||The paramedics gave first aid to the fire survivor and then transported him to the hospital.||
