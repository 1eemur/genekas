**Tänane WOTD**
Ehitama - Tegusõna/Verb
*ehitama, ehitada; ehitab, ehitas; ehitanud, ehitatud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** hoonet vm ehitist püstitama või rajama,osadest kokku panema või valmistama,erinevatest osadest midagi tegema,eriti mingit ehitist püstitama
🇪🇪 **2.** mingit seisukohta, suhtumist vms kujundama või looma
🇬🇧 **1.** to erect or build a building etc., to assemble or make from parts, to make something out of different parts, especially to erect some kind of building
🇬🇧 **2.** to form or create any point of view, attitude, etc

**Näited/Examples:**
● Viiekorruseline maja ehitati valmis vaid viie kuuga. ||The five-story building was completed in just five months.||
● Kiireid autosid ehitati ka juba ligemale 100 aastat tagasi. ||Fast cars were also built almost 100 years ago.||
● Mees ehitas perele maja. ||The man built a house for the family.||
● Hoone ehitati betoonist. ||The building was built of concrete.||
● Presidendikampaania on ehitatud lubadusele tuua valitsemisse muutusi. ||The presidential campaign is built on a promise to bring change to governance.||
