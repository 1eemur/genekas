Heastama - Tegusõna/Verb
*heastama, heastada; heastab, heastas; heastanud, heastatud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** halba heaks pöörama,korvama, kompenseerima
🇬🇧 **1.** to turn bad into good, to make up, to compensate

**Näited/Examples:**
● Sportlane püüdis varasemaid ebaõnnestumisi heastada. ||The athlete tried to make up for their previous failures.||
● Ettevõte on avaldanud tahet kahju heastada. ||The company has expressed its willingness to make amends for the damage.||
● Mõrtsuka ohvrile kahju heastada pole niikuinii võimalik , sest ta on surnud.  ||It is impossible to compensate the murderer's victim, because he is dead.||
● Peamine eeldus on siiras soov heastada kannatanule tekitatud kahju. ||The main prerequisite is a sincere desire to compensate the victim for the damage caused.||
● Turbapõlenguga tekitatud kahju heastatakse valitsuse reservfondist. ||The damage caused by the peat fire will be compensated from the government's reserve fund.||
