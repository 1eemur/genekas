**Tänane WOTD**
Kiirabi - Nimisõna/Noun
*kiirabi, kiirabi, kiirabi; -, -, -*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** erakorralise meditsiiniabi andmine väljaspool haiglat, vajaduse korral ka haige transportimine haiglasse,sellist abi andev asutus,töötajad, kes viivad haigeid või vigastatud inimesi haiglasse või annavad kohapeal abi,vastav sõiduk
🇪🇪 **2.** erakorralist meditsiiniabi andev kiirabibrigaad,kiirabiauto
🇬🇧 **1.** providing emergency medical care outside the hospital, if necessary also transporting the patient to the hospital, the institution providing such care, employees who take sick or injured people to the hospital or provide help on the spot, the appropriate vehicle
🇬🇧 **2.** ambulance brigade providing emergency medical assistance, ambulance

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kukkunud vanainimene vajas kiirabi. ||An old man who had fallen needed an ambulance.||
● Mare on aastaid kiirabis töötanud. ||Mare has worked in the ambulance for many years.||
● Ta viidi haiglasse kiirabiga. ||He was taken to the hospital by ambulance.||
● Helistasin kiirabisse. ||I called an ambulance||
● Tules sai kannatada korteri omanik, kellele kiirabi andis esmaabi. ||The owner of the apartment was injured in the fire and was given first aid by the ambulance crew.||
● Kannatanu viidi kiirabiga haiglasse. ||The victim was taken to the hospital by ambulance.||
