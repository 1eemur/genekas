**Tänane WOTD**
Aku - Nimisõna/Noun
*aku, aku, akut; akud, akude, akusid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** energia talletamise seade, mida saab pärast tühjenemast uuesti laadida
🇬🇧 **1.** battery, an energy storage device that can be recharged after being discharged

**Näited/Examples:**
● Aku on täis. ||The battery is full.||
● Unustasin telefoni aku laadima panna. ||I forgot to charge my phone.||
● Mobiiltelefoni aku oli tühjaks saamas. ||The mobile phone's battery was getting low.||
● Aku kestab pikalt ja laadida on vaja harva. ||The battery lasts a long time and rarely needs recharging.||
● Telefon on aeglane ja aku liialt lühikese kestvusega. ||The phone is slow and the battery life is too short.||
