**Tänane WOTD**
Meelega - Määrsõna/Adverb
*meelega*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 kindla kavatsusega, mitte kogemata 
🇬🇧 deliberately, on purpose, with specific intent, not by accident 

**Näited/Examples:**
● Nad ei põhjustaks mitte kunagi teistele meelega kannatusi. ||They would never intentionally hurt one another.||
● Sa ju tead, et ma ei teinud seda meelega. ||You know I didn't do it deliberately.||
● Ma pean tõestama, et laev juhiti meelega kõrvale. ||I have to prove that ship was deliberately sent off course.||
● Tegin seda meelega. ||I did this intentionally.||
