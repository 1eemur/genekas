**Tänane WOTD**
Ahi - Nimisõna/Noun
*ahi, ahju, ahju; ahjud, ahjude, ahje/ahjusid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** kinnine tulekolle ruumi(de) kütmiseks,seade, mille sees põletatakse puid, et sellega ruume kütta
🇪🇪 **2.** pliidi sisse ehitatud kinnine kamber toidu küpsetamiseks,mikrolaineahi, grillahi vms,pliidi osa, mille sees saab toitu valmistada ja millel on uks ees
🇬🇧 **1.** stove, a closed fireplace for heating a room(s), a device in which wood is burned to heat the rooms
🇬🇧 **2.** oven, a closed chamber built into a stove for cooking food, a microwave oven, a grill, etc., a part of a stove in which food can be cooked and which has a door in front

**Näited/Examples:**
● Tegin tule ahju. ||I made a fire in the stove.||
● Ahi ajab suitsu sisse. ||The stove is emitting smoke.||
● Saunaahi. ||Sauna stove.||
● Vanaema võttis ahjust õunakoogi. ||Grandma took an apple cake out of the oven.||
● Panin leiva ahju. ||I put the bread in the oven.||
● Liha on ahjus. ||The meat is in the oven.||
