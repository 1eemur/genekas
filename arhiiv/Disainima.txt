**Tänane WOTD**
Disainima - Tegusõna/Verb
*Disainima, Disainida; Disainib, Disainis; Disaininud, Disainitud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** tarbeesemeid, rõivaid vm tooteid ja teenuseid loovalt arendama ja kujundama
🇬🇧 **1.** to creatively develop and design consumer goods, clothing, etc. products and services

**Näited/Examples:**
● Ta disainib oma riided ise. ||She designs her own clothes.||
● Kunstitudengite disainitud mööbel. ||Furniture designed by art students.||
● Mis tunne oli esimest korda meestele disainida? ||What was it like to design for men for the first time?||
● Stsenaariumid disainiti 2011. aasta jaanuarist kuni märtsini. ||The scenarios were designed from January to March in 2011.||
● Kunstnik disainib ja toodab rõivaid, kotte ja muid aksessuaare. ||The artist designs and manufactures clothes, bags and other accessories.||
