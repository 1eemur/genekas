Keskealine - Omadussõna/Adjective
*keskealine, keskealise, keskealist; keskealised, keskealiste, keskealiseid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** (inimese kohta:) keskeas olev,selline, kes vanuse poolest jääb noore ja vana vahele
🇪🇪 **2.** sellises vanuses inimene
🇪🇪 **3.** (looduse, taime vm kohta:) ealt keskmine
🇬🇧 **1.** (of a person) middle-aged, someone who is between young and old in age
🇬🇧 **2.** a person of that age
🇬🇧 **3.** (about nature, plant, etc.) of average age


**Näited/Examples:**
● Keskealiste öölokaal. ||A nightclub for middle-aged people.||
● Kokkupõrkes hukkus keskealine mees. ||A middle-aged man died in the collision.||
● Keskealine abielupaar. Kokkupõrkes hukkus keskealine mees.||A middle-aged married couple.||
● Saalis istusid valdavalt keskealised inimesed. ||It was mostly middle-aged people sitting in the hall.||
● Keskealine männik. ||Middle-aged pine tree.||
