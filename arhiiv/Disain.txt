**Tänane WOTD**
Disain - Nimisõna/Noun
*disain, disaini, disaini; disainid, disainide, disaine/disainisid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** toodete ja teenuste (nt tarbeesemete, rõivaste, veebikeskkonna) loov ja hrl tehnoloogiliselt uuenduslik arendamine ja kujundamine
🇪🇪 **2.** toodete väliskujundus
🇬🇧 **1.** design, creative and technologically innovative development and design of products and services (e.g. consumer goods, clothing, web environment)
🇬🇧 **2.** design, exterior design of products

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kontsern tegeleb naisterõivaste disaini, tootmise ja turustamisega. ||The group is engaged in the design, production and distribution of women's clothing.||
● Mööblidisain. ||Furniture design.||
● Ülimoodsa disainiga rõivad. ||Clothing with ultra-modern design.||
● Üldpildis on Eesti disain skandinaavialiku lähenemisega. ||Overall, Estonian design has a Scandinavian approach.||
● Silikoonümbrisega on disainilt ilus telefon muudetud koledaks känkariks. ||With a silicone case, a phone that is beautiful in design has been turned into an ugly hulk.||
