**Tänane WOTD**
Kohvimasin - Nimisõna/Noun
*kohvimasin, kohvimasina, kohvimasinat; kohvimasinad, kohvimasinate, kohvimasinaid*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 kohvi tegemiseks mõeldud aparaat 
🇬🇧 coffee machine, a coffee-making apparatus

**Näited/Examples:**
● Mu kohvimasin on katki ja ma ei oska seda parandada. ||My coffee machine is broken and I can't fix it.||
● Veekeetjad ja kohvimasinad on levinud nii kodudes kui ka kontorites. ||Kettles and coffee machines are common in homes and offices.||
● Panin kohvimasinasse vett ja kohviube ning vajutasin lülitile. ||I put water and coffee beans in the coffee machine and pressed the switch.||
● Nad keetsid kohvi elektripliidil, kuna kohvimasin läks katki. ||They were making coffee on an electric stove because the coffee machine broke.||
● Kontorisse osteti uus kohvimasin, millega saab ka espressot ja kakaod teha. ||A new coffee machine was bought for the office, which can also make espresso and cocoa.||
 
**Sünonüümid/Synonyms:**
