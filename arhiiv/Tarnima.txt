**Tänane WOTD**
Tarnima - Tegusõna/Verb
*tarnima, tarnida; tarnib, tarnis; tarninud, tarnitud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** millegagi varustama, midagi kellelegi müüma, pakkuma või kasutada andma
🇬🇧 **1.** to provide something, sell, offer or use something to give something

**Näited/Examples:**
● Riik tarnib ülestõusnutele relvi. ||The state supplies arms to the rebels.||
● Firma tarnib ja paigaldab mööblit. ||The company supplies and installs furniture.||
● Lepingu kohaselt tarnib firma seadmed jaanuaris. ||According to the contract, the company will supply the equipment in January.||
● Rootsi omakorda tarnib elektrit Leetu, Poolasse, Saksamaale ja Taani. ||Sweden, for its part, supplies electricity to Lithuania, Poland, Germany and Denmark.||
● Tarkvara tarnitakse elektroonselt, saate selle alla laadida kohe pärast makse sooritamist. ||The software is delivered electronically, you can download it immediately after payment.||
