Vaigistama - Tegusõna/Verb
*vaigistama, vaigistada; vaigistab, vaigistas; vaigistanud, vaigistatud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** rahustama, rahunema sundima,maha suruma
🇪🇪 **2.** häält, heli vaikse(ma)ks tegema
🇪🇪 **3.** (tunde, aistingu vms kohta:) leevendama, kustutama
🇬🇧 **1.** to reassure, to calm down, to suppress
🇬🇧 **2.** to quiet a voice, make it more quiet)
🇬🇧 **3.** (of a feeling, sensation, etc.) to relieve, to erase.

**Näited/Examples:**
● Politseisse helistamine vaigistas ähvardajad hetkega. ||A call to the police silenced the threats in an instant.||
● Sügav hingamine vaigistab meele. ||Deep breathing quiets the mind.||
● Skandaali ei õnnestunud vaigistada. ||The scandal could not be silenced.||
● Ta vaigistas oma hääle sosinaks. ||He lowered his voice to a whisper.||
● Rahva viha üritati vaigistada. ||The anger of the people was tried to silence.||
● Lavendliõli aitab peavalu vaigistada. ||Lavender oil helps to silence headaches.||
