**Tänane WOTD**
Tomat - Nimisõna/Noun
*tomat, tomati, tomatit; tomatid, tomatite, tomateid*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** tomatitaime vili toiduna   
🇪🇪 **1.1** köögiviljana kasvatatav peamiselt punaste lihakate viljadega rohttaim  
🇬🇧 **1.** tomato, a glossy red, or occasionally yellow, pulpy edible fruit that is eaten as a vegetable or in salad
🇬🇧 **1.1** the South American plant of the nightshade family that produces the tomato. It is widely grown as a cash crop and many varieties have been developed

**Näited/Examples:**
● Päikesekuivatatud tomatid. ||Sun-dried tomatoes.||
● Tema ütleb tomat, mina ütlen sibul. ||She says tomato, I say onion.||
● Kas tomat on puu- või juurvili? ||Is a tomato a fruit or a vegetable?||
● Ja nüüd oli ta näost punane nagu tomat. ||And now her face was as red as a tomato.||
● Pudel õli, üks tomat, küüslauk, peotäis pipart. ||A bottle of oil, one tomato, garlic, a fistful of pepper.||
