**Tänane WOTD**
Intelligentne - Omadussõna/Adjective
*intelligentne, intelligentse, intelligentset; intelligentsed, intelligentsete, intelligentseid*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 arenenud, uuriva ja terase mõistusega, keerulisi ideid, olukordi mõistev, heade teadmistega  
🇬🇧 intelligent, well-developed, inquiring and perceptive mind, able to grasp complex ideas and situations, with good knowledge 

**Näited/Examples:**
● Ta on väga intelligentne. ||She's very intelligent.||
● Mulle meeldivad intelligentsed mehed. ||I like intelligent men.||
● Delfiinid on väga intelligentsed. ||Dolphins are very intelligent.||
● Nad on vooruslikud ja eeskujulikud, intelligentsed ja töökad. ||They are virtuous and exemplary, intelligent and industrious.||
● Ma olen alati öelnud, et te olete sama intelligentne kui kaunis. ||I have always said that you are as intelligent as you are beautiful. ||
