Sobimatu - Omadussõna/Adjective
*sobimatu, sobimatu, sobimatut; sobimatud, sobimatute, sobimatuid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** millekski mitte (hästi) kõlbav, mingitele tingimustele mittevastav,selline, mis ei sobi millekski või mis ei vasta mingitele tingimustele
🇬🇧 **1.** not suitable (well) for anything, not meeting certain conditions, such that it is not suitable for anything or does not meet certain conditions

**Näited/Examples:**
● Sobimatud ilmastikuolud. ||Inappropriate weather conditions.||
● Teata sobimatust kommentaarist! ||Report an inappropriate comment!||
● Täiesti sobimatu inimene sellele ametikohale. ||A completely inappropriate person for this post.||
● Uue raekoja asukoht on täiesti sobimatu. ||The location of the new town hall is completely unsuitable.||
● Kuidas teha vahet sobival ja sobimatul projektiideel? ||How can we distinguish between appropriate and inappropriate project ideas?||
