Restoran - Nimisõna/Noun
*restoran, restorani, restorani; restoranid, restoranide, restorane*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** söögi-, joogi- ja ajaviitekoht, kus hrl toite valmistatakse kohapeal
🇬🇧 **1.** restaurant, a place to eat, drink and spend time, usually where food is prepared on site

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kiirtoidurestoran. ||Fast food restaurant.||
● Pitsarestoran. ||Pizza restaurant.||
● Kalarestoran. ||Fish restaurant.||
● Küla on tuntud heade kalarestoranide poolest. ||The village is known for its good fish restaurants.||
● Linn on täis restorane ja kohvikuid. ||The city is full of restaurants and cafés.||
