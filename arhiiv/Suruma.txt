Suruma - Tegusõna/Verb
*Suruma, Suruda; Surub, Surus; Surunud, Surutud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** oma raskusega, survega millelegi vajutama, midagi (mingisse asendisse või mingis suunas) pressima
🇪🇪 **2.** (kuhugi toppides või pistes, ka vaevaga mahutades)
🇪🇪 **3.** (hillitsetud, vihase, vaevalise rääkimise kohta)
🇪🇪 **4.** (võrkpallis:) ettetõstetud palli järsu alla suunatud löögiga üle võrgu lööma, ründelööki sooritama
🇪🇪 **5.** pingutama; 
🇪🇪 **6.** summutama, takistama, tagasi hoidma
🇪🇪 **7.** jõudu, mõju rakendades midagi läbi viima, tegema
🇬🇧 **1.** to press something with one's weight, pressure, to press something (into a certain position or in a certain direction)
🇬🇧 **2.** (by stuffing or tucking in somewhere, also fitting with difficulty)
🇬🇧 **3.** (about speaking in a slurred, angry, difficult manner)
🇬🇧 **4.** (in volleyball) to hit the raised ball over the net with a sharp downward shot, to perform an attack shot
🇬🇧 **5.** make an effort
🇬🇧 **6.** to suppress, prevent, hold back
🇬🇧 **7.** to carry out something by applying force, influence

**Näited/Examples:**
● Poiss surus mütsi sügavasti pähe. ||The boy pressed his hat deeply on his head.||
● Naabrinaine surus kõrva vastu ust, et paremini kuulda. ||The neighbor woman pressed her ear against the door to hear better.||
● Poisike surub sõrmed rusikasse. ||The little boy clenches his fingers into a fist.||
● Haige surub sõnu vaevaliselt suust. ||The patient forces the words out of his mouth with difficulty.||
● Talle on liiga palju kohustusi kaela surutud. ||Too many responsibilities have been thrust upon him.||
● Tasapisi õnnestus konkurendid kõrvale suruda. ||Little by little, it was possible to push the competitors aside.||
