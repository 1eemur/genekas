**Tänane WOTD**
Raba - Nimisõna/Noun
*raba, raba, raba; rabad, rabade, rabu/rabasid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** suuremate soomassiivide keskosas asuv ümbruse pinnast kõrgem ala, milles ladestub kasvav turbakiht ning kus taimed toituvad üksnes sademetest,ümbruse pinnast kõrgemale ulatuv väikeste veekogudega soo
🇬🇧 **1.** bog, an area located in the middle of larger marsh above the surrounding surface, in which a growing peat layer is deposited and where plants feed only on precipitation, a marsh with small bodies of water extending above the surrounding surface

**Näited/Examples:**
● Käisime rabas matkal. ||We went hiking in the bog.||
● Rabasse ehitati laudtee. ||A boardwalk was built in the bog.||
● Raba peale langes tihe udu. ||A thick fog descended on the bog.||
● Rabade ja soode ilu on lummav ja maagiline. ||The beauty of bogs and swamps is enchanting and magical.||
● Vaatetorni juurest suundub matkarada raba peale.  ||A hiking trail leads from the observation tower to the bog.||
