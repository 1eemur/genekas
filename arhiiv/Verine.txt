Verine - Omadussõna/Adjective
*verine, verise, verist; verised, veriste, veriseid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** verega koos, verd jooksev, veritsev,verd sisaldav, veresegune,selline, kus on verd, verega koos
🇪🇪 **2.** selline, kus on (palju) tapmist, verevalamist, rohkete ohvritega,selline, kus on palju tapmist ja ohvreid
🇪🇪 **3.** (paljude veretöödega seotud inimese kohta)
🇪🇪 **4.** vihane, tige,raevukas
🇪🇪 **5.** (määra väljendades:) väga suur, äärmine
🇬🇧 **1.** with blood, bleeding, bleeding, containing blood, mixed with blood, with blood, with blood
🇬🇧 **2.** one with (a lot of) killing, bloodshed, with lots of victims, one with a lot of killing and victims
🇬🇧 **3.** (about a person involved in many bloody deeds)
🇬🇧 **4.** angry, vicious, furious
🇬🇧 **5.** (expressing rate) very large, extreme

**Näited/Examples:**
● Maas lebas verine mees. ||A bloody man lay on the ground.||
● Su põlv on verine. ||Your knee is bloody.||
● Mehel oli nina verine. ||The man had a bloody nose.||
● All-linna tänavatel peeti veriseid võitlusi. ||Bloody battles were fought in the streets of the lower city.||
● Verised vallutajad. ||Bloody conquerors.||
