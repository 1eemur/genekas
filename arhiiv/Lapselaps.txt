**Tänane WOTD**
Lapselaps - Nimisõna/Noun
*lapselaps, lapselapse, lapselast; lapselapsed, lapselaste, lapselapsi*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 poja või tütre laps  
🇬🇧 grandchild, child of a son or daughter

**Näited/Examples:**
● Vanaema koob lapselapsele jõuluks salli.||A grandmother knits a scarf for her grandchild for Christmas.||
● Teadsin, et tal oli õigus: Adele ei olnud minu lapselaps. ||I knew he was right: Adele was not my granddaughter.||
● Lapselapsed kuulasid hämminguga vanaisa jutte. ||The grandchildren listened with amazement to their grandfather's stories.||
● Lapselaps poetab mokaotsast, et vanaisa nagunii ei saa aru, mis" äpp" on. ||The grandchild mutters that grandpa doesn't understand what an "app" is anyway.||
● Mõnedes kultuurides lapselapsele antav vanaisa nimi väljendab uskumust, et lapselaps on oma vanaisa taaskehastus. ||In some cultures, the grandfather name given to a grandchild expresses the belief that the grandchild is the reincarnation of his grandfather.||
