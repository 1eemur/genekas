Sinakas - Omadussõna/Adjective
*sinakas, sinaka, sinakat; sinakad, sinakate, sinakaid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** veidi sinine, sinise varjundiga
🇬🇧 **1.** bluish, a little blue, with a blue tint

**Näited/Examples:**
● Sinakas valgus. ||Bluish light.||
● Jää on ilusa sinaka varjundiga. ||The ice has a beautiful bluish tint.||
● Metsa kohal hõljus sinakas suitsuvine. ||A bluish plume of smoke floated over the forest.||
● Kosmosest paistab Maa helendava sinaka kettana. ||From space, the Earth appears as a glowing blue disc.||
● Happelises mullas kasvades muutuvad õied sinakaks. ||When growing in acidic soil, the blossoms turn bluish.||

**Sünonüümid/Synonyms:** 
sinkjas, sinisevõitu
