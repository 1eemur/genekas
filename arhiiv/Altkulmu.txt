**Tänane WOTD**
Altkulmu - Määrsõna/Adverb
*altkulmu*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 kortsutatud kulmude alt, hrl ebasõbralikult, umbusklikult 
🇬🇧 from beneath wrinkled eyebrows, usually unfriendly, suspiciously 

**Näited/Examples:**
● Poiss heitis mulle altkulmu pilgu. ||The boy gave me a furrowed look.|| 
● Vastaspingil istuv mees jälgis teda altkulmu. ||The man sitting on the opposite bench watched him from beneath lowered eyebrows.|| 
● Sa oleksid pidanud mind uskuma, mõtles ta mehele altkulmu otsa vaadates. ||You should have believed me, she thought, staring at the man from beneath lowered brows.|| 
● „Tulin sinu juurde, kurjuse isand ja varjude käskija,” vastas mees ja vaatas altkulmu ebasõbralikult Wolandi poole. ||‘I have come to see you, spirit of evil and lord of the shadows,’ the man replied with a hostile glare at Woland.||
