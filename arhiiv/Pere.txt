Pere - Nimisõna/Noun
*pere, pere, peret; pered, perede, peresid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** ühe majandusliku üksusena elavad vanemad ja lapsed,(laiemalt hõlmab ka lähisugulasi),rühm inimesi, kes elavad koos ja on omavahel sugulased, tavaliselt vanemad ja lapsed
🇪🇪 **2.** (endisajal:) talu pererahvas koos sulasrahvaga, ka samas elavad pererahva sugulased
🇪🇪 **3.** rühm inimesi, kes kuuluvad kokku nt ühise tegevuse või ühiste huvide alusel,rühm sama algupära rahvaid
🇪🇪 **4.** rühm esemeid, asju, nähtusi, millel on ühiseid iseloomulikke omadusi või mis on sama algupära
🇪🇪 **5.** ühe pesa (hrl taru) mesilaste, harvemini kuklaste kogum
🇬🇧 **1.** family parents and children living as one unit, (broadly also includes close relatives), a group of people who live together and are related to each other, usually parents and children
🇬🇧 **2.** (in former times) the family of the farm together with the servants, relatives of the family also live there
🇬🇧 **3.** a group of people who belong together, e.g. on the basis of common activities or common interests, a group of peoples of the same origin
🇬🇧 **4.** a group of objects, things, phenomena that have common characteristic properties or have the same origin
🇬🇧 **5.** a collection of bees, in one hive

**Näited/Examples:**
● Lasterikkad pered. ||Families with many children.||
● Mitu liiget teie peres on? ||How many members are there in your family?||
● Nende peres on viis last. ||There are five children in their family.||
● Terve pere jäi haigeks. ||The whole family got sick.||
● Soome-ugri rahvaste pere. ||The family of Finno-Ugric peoples.||
● Linnamaasturite perre kuuluv auto. ||A car belonging to the family of SUVs.||
