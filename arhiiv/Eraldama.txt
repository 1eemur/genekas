**Tänane WOTD**
Eraldama - Tegusõna/Verb
*eraldama, eraldada; eraldab, eraldas; eraldanud, eraldatud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** mingiks otstarbeks suunama, kellelegi kasutada andma (nt raha, maad, tuba),raha või muid vahendeid kellelegi kasutada andma
🇪🇪 **2.** mingit osa tervikust eemaldama, millestki välja suunama
🇪🇪 **3.** üksteisest lahus hoidma või lahutama, ümbritsevast eraldi hoidma,millegi vahel olema,millegi või kellegi vahel olema, neid üksteisest lahutama
🇪🇪 **4.** millegi vahel erinevust nägema, vahet tegema, erinevaks tunnistama, erinevana tunnetama,erinevust nägema, vahet tegema,lahus hoidma
🇬🇧 **1.** to direct for some purpose, to give someone to use (e.g. money, land, a room), to give money or other means to someone to use
🇬🇧 **2.** to remove some part of the whole, to direct out of something
🇬🇧 **3.** to keep apart or separate from each other, keep separate from the surroundings, be between something, be between something or someone, separate them from each other
🇬🇧 **4.** to see the difference between something, to differentiate, to recognize as different, to feel different, to see the difference, to distinguish, to keep apart

**Näited/Examples:**
● Valitsus eraldas raha neljale tööhõive projektile. ||The government allocated funds for four employment projects.||
● Ministeerium eraldas projektile kuus tuhat eurot. ||The Ministry allocated six thousand euros to the project.||
● Filter eraldab veest kahjulikud ained. ||The filter separates harmful substances from the water.||
● Lühikeste kriipsudega katkendjoon eraldab suunavööndeid kaherajalisel teel. ||A dashed line with short dashes separates the sides of the road on a two-lane road.||
● Eestit ja Rootsit eraldab Läänemeri. ||Estonia and Sweden are separated by the Baltic Sea.||
