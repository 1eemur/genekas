**Tänane WOTD**
Kahvel - Nimisõna/Noun
*kahvel, kahvli, kahvlit; kahvlid, kahvlite, kahvleid*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** mitme terava haruga söögi- või serveerimisriist 
🇪🇪 **1.1** kahe haruga seade (nt jalgrattal) või mingi harudega tööriist 
🇪🇪 **1.2** purjelaeva masti küljes olev kaheharuline põikpuu, mille külge kinnitatakse nelinurkse pikipurje ülaäär  
🇪🇪 **1.3** (mingi vahemiku, muutumisruumi kohta)  
🇪🇪 **2.** (males:) olukord, kus ratsu, ettur vm malend tulistab korraga kaht vigurit  
🇬🇧 **1.** fork, a cutlery or serving utensil with several sharp points
🇬🇧 **1.1** a device with two branches (e.g. on a bicycle) or a tool with two branches
🇬🇧 **1.2** gaff, a two-branched spar attached to the mast of a sailing vessel, to which the upper end of a square long-haul sail is attached
🇬🇧 **1.3** (for a range, a margin of variation)
🇬🇧 **2.** (in chess) a fork, a tactic in which a piece attacks multiple enemy pieces simultaneously

**Näited/Examples:**
● Torgi kahvliga taignasse augud sisse. ||Poke holes in the dough with a fork.||
● Suru banaanid kahvliga püreeks. ||Mash the bananas into a puree with a fork.||
● Ema pani lauale kaks tassi, taldrikud, noad ja kahvlid. ||Mom put two cups, plates, knives, and forks on the table.||
● Ta teab mis kahvliga salatit süüa, seega tehakse ta kapteniks. ||He knows which fork to use to eat a salad, so he's made captain.||
● Kahvel, lipp kukub! ||A fork, the queen is falling!||
