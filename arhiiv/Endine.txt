**Tänane WOTD**
Endine - Omadussõna/Adjective
*endine, endise, endist; endised, endiste, endisi*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** varasemasse, möödunud aega kuuluv, ennemini (keegi) olnud, kellenagi töötanud vms
🇪🇪 **2.** selline nagu varem, muutumatult sama
🇬🇧 **1.** former, belonging to an earlier, past time, being (someone) before, working for someone, etc
🇬🇧 **2.** the same as before, unchanged

**Näited/Examples:**
● Mart on endine vähiarst. ||Mart is a former cancer doctor.||
● Briti endine koloonia. ||A former British colony.||
● Kardame, et kõik jätkub endist viisi. ||We fear that everything will continue as before.||
● Maja taastati endisel kujul. ||The house was restored to its former form.||
● Tase on endine. ||The level is the same as before.||
