**Tänane WOTD**
Hernes - Nimisõna/Noun
*hernes, herne, hernest; herned, herneste, herneid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** väikesi ümmargusi seemneid sisaldavate kaunviljade ja liblika tiibu meenutavate õitega rohttaim,taim, millel on väikeste ümmarguste roheliste seemnetega viljad
🇪🇪 **2.** hernetaime seeme (koos kaunaga), ka toiduna,väike ümmargune roheline seeme, mida on ühe pika vilja sees mitmeid
🇪🇪 **3.** hernepõld või -peenar
🇬🇧 **1.** pea, a herbaceous plant with legumes containing small round seeds and flowers resembling butterfly wings, a plant that has fruits with small round green seeds
🇬🇧 **2.** pea plant seed (with pod), also used as food, small round green seed, several of which are contained within a long fruit
🇬🇧 **3.** pea field or bed


**Näited/Examples:**
● Peotäis herneid. ||A handful of peas.||
● Ostsin turult kilo värskeid herneid. ||I bought a kilo of fresh peas at the market.||
● Külvasime peenrale hernest ja porgandit. ||We sowed peas and carrots in the garden bed.||
● Harilik hernes jaguneb põldherneks ning aedherneks. ||Peas are divided into field peas and garden peas.||
● Lõika sink ribadeks ja sega keedetud pasta, herneste ja brokoliga. ||Cut the ham into strips and mix with the cooked pasta, peas and broccoli.||
