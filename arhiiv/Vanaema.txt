**Tänane WOTD**
Vanaema - Nimisõna/Noun
*vanaema, vanaema, vanaema; vanaemad, vanaemade, vanaemasid*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** isa ema või ema ema 
🇪🇪 **1.1** (südamlikult vana naise kohta)
🇪🇪 **1.2** naine kui millelegi alusepanija, millegi esindaja vms
🇬🇧 **1.** grandmother, the mother of a father or mother
🇬🇧 **1.1** (affectionately about an old woman)
🇬🇧 **1.2** a woman as the founder of something, the representative of something

**Näited/Examples:**
● Vanaema kudus kampsuni. ||Grandma knitted a sweater.||
● Ta on juba kolmekordne vanaema. ||She is already a grandmother of three.||
● Poiss elas isapoolse vanaema juures. ||The boy lived with his paternal grandmother.||
● Vanaema koob lapselapsele jõuluks salli. ||Grandmother knits a scarf for her grandchild for Christmas.||
● Kas vastab tõele, et hoki juurde juhatas teid vanaema? ||Is it true that your grandmother introduced you to hockey?||
