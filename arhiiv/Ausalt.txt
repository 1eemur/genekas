**Tänane WOTD**
Ausalt - Määrsõna/Adverb
*ausalt*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** tõest ja õiglusest juhindudes, ilma valetamata; lepetest ja lubadustest kinni pidades  
🇪🇪 **1.1** (kõnekeelne) (ütlusena suhtluses:) (päris) tõesti, tõsijutt  
🇬🇧 **1.** honestly, truly, guided by truth and justice, without lying; keeping agreements and promises
🇬🇧 **1.1** (colloquial) (as a saying in communication) (really) really, the true story

**Näited/Examples:**
● On aeg hakata ausalt asju ajama. ||It is time to start doing things honestly.||
● Mõtle ja vasta küsimusele ausalt. ||Think and answer the question honestly.||
● Ausalt öelda ma ei tea, kas tahan seda leida. ||To be honest, I don't know if I wanna find it.||
● Ta tunnistas ausalt ja avalikult oma raskusi. ||He acknowledged his difficulties honestly and publicly.||
● Tavalisele inimesele on tähtis see, et linna juhitakse ausalt ja hästi. ||It is important for the ordinary person that the city is run honestly and well.||
