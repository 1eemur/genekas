**Tänane WOTD**
Leevendama - Tegusõna/Verb
*leevendama, leevendada; leevendab, leevendas; leevendanud, leevendatud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** midagi raskesti talutavat, ebameeldivat vähendama või nõrgendama, seda talutavamaks muutma
🇬🇧 **1.** to reduce or attenuate something intolerable, unpleasant, make it more tolerable

**Näited/Examples:**
● Valu leevendati ravimitega. ||The pain was relieved with drugs.||
● Alkohol leevendab stressi. ||Alcohol relieves stress. (not a recommendation)||
● Õnneks on tõusnud tuul, mis veidi leevendab leitsakut. ||Fortunately, the wind has risen, which alleviates the heats a bit.||
● Mitmed riigid on aidanud leevendada põgenike olukorda. ||Many countries have helped to alleviate the situation of refugees.||
● Karjäär viis tippu, aga seegi ei leevendanud tema üksindust. ||A career took her to the top, but even that didn't ease her loneliness.||
