**Tänane WOTD**
Kink - Nimisõna/Noun
*kink, kingi, kinki; kingid, kinkike, kinke/kinkisid*
*Lühike Sisseütlev: kinki*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 kingitav või kingitud ese, vara vms 
🇬🇧 gift, a gift or donation of an item, property, etc. 

**Näited/Examples:**
● See oli täiuslik kink. ||It was the perfect gift.||
● Kas sa said mu kingi kätte? || Did you get my present?||
● See oli kink tema isalt. ||It was a present from her father.||
● Jõuluvana teab täpselt, mida ma kingiks tahan. ||Santa knows exactly what I want as a present.||
● Iga laps sai jõuluvanalt kingiks kommipaki. ||Each child received a packet of candy as a present from Santa.||
● Vanematele ostan kingid tõenäoliselt sel nädalavahetusel. ||I'll probably buy presents for the parents this weekend.||
 
**Sünonüümid/Synonyms:** 
kingitus (more common)
