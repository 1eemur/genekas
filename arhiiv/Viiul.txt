**Tänane WOTD**
Viiul - Nimisõna/Noun
*viiul, viiuli, viiulit; viiulid, viiulite, viiuleid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** poognaga (harvemini sõrmitsedes) mängitav väike nelja keelega pill
🇬🇧 **1.** violin, a small instrument with four strings played with a bow (less often with the fingers).

**Näited/Examples:**
● Stradivari viiul. ||Stradivari violin.||
● Mari musitseerib viiulil ja Jüri klaveril. ||Mari plays the violin and Jüri plays the piano.||
● Laagris õpetatakse lastele viiuli ja kandle mängimist. ||Children are taught to play the violin and the zither at the camp.||
● Õppisin Rootsis klassikalist viiulit ning mängisin orkestris. ||I studied classical violin in Sweden and played in an orchestra.||
● Sofia mängib viiulit ja õpetab seda ka Vanalinna Muusikamajas lastele. ||Sofia plays the violin and also teaches it to children at the Old Town Music House.||
