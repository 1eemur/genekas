**Tänane WOTD**
Seevastu  - Määrsõna/Adverb
*seevastu*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 nagu vastukaaluks, tasakaalustuseks, hüvituseks millelegi eespool mainitule 
🇬🇧 on the contrary, as a counterbalance, a counterweight, a compensation for something mentioned above

**Näited/Examples:**
● Jaanuar oli külm, veebruar seevastu üllatavalt soe. ||January was cold, on the other hand February was surprisingly warm.||
● Naiste mured seevastu kuhjuvad kogu eluea vältel. ||Women's concerns, on the other hand, accumulate throughout their whole life.||
● Riigikaitse ülesehitamine võtab aastaid, lõhkumine seevastu aga ainult kuid. ||It takes years to build up a national defence, but only months to destroy it.||
● Populaarsust on seevastu kogunud katsed varastada sotsiaalmeedia kontode paroole. ||Attempts to steal passwords to social media accounts, on the other hand, have gained popularity.||
● Enesekindlat inimest võidakse pidada liiga agressiivseks, kinnist inimest seevastu veidi salakavalaks. ||A confident person may be seen as overly aggressive, while on the other hand, a reserved person may be seen as a little devious.||
 
**Sünonüümid/Synonyms:** 
see-eest, selle-eest, sellevastu, seeeest, selleeest
