**Tänane WOTD**
Lummav - Omadussõna/Adjective
*lummav, lummava, lummavat; lummavad, lummavate, lummavaid*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 meeldivuse või eripäraga kütkestav, oma lummusesse haarav, väga kaunis või meeldiv 
🇬🇧 captivating in terms of its pleasantness or special features, charming, enchanting, very beautiful or pleasing to the eye

**Näited/Examples:**
● Ma loodan, et su raamat on lummav. ||I hope your book is captivating.||
● Iga naise liigutus paistis lummav. ||Every movement of hers seemed fascinating.||
● Nende mitmekesisus ja keerukus on lummav. ||Their variety and complexity is breathtaking.||
● See on lummav hotell oma vaikse baariga. ||It's a charming hotel with a quiet bar.||
●  Lubage mul selgitada, sest tegelikult on see üsnagi lummav. ||Allow me to explain because it's quite fascinating, actually.||
