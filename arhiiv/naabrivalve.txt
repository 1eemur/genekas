
**Tänane WOTD**
Naabrivalve - Nimisõna/Noun
*naabrivalve, naabrivalve, naabrivalvet; naabrivalved, naabrivalvete, naabrivalveid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** sama piirkonna elanike ühine järelevalve kodu ja naabruskonna üle
🇬🇧 **1.** joint surveillance of home and neighborhood by residents of the same area

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kas maal on toimiv naabrivalve? ||Is neighborhood watch effective in rural areas?||
● Sattusin naabrivalve huviorbiiti. ||I came into the interest of the neighborhood watch.||
● Turvalisuse tagamiseks külas on loodud naabrivalve sektor. ||A neighborhood watch sector has been established in the village to ensure safety.||
● Tavaline naabrivalve süsteem eramajade rajoonides hästi ei toimi. ||The standard neighborhood watch system does not work well in areas with private homes.||
● Esimese etapina koguvad ühistud kokku aktiivsed majaelanikud, kes sooviksid naabrivalves osaleda. ||As a first step, housing associations gather active residents who would like to participate in the neighborhood watch.||
