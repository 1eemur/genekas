**Tänane WOTD**
Pulber - Nimisõna/Noun
*pulber, pulbri, pulbrit; pulbrid, pulbrite, pulbreid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** peenestatud tahke aine,peeneks tehtud tahke aine
🇪🇪 **2.** selline kindlas koguses pakitud, hrl vees lahustatav ravim või preparaat
🇬🇧 **1.** powder, pulverized solid substance, finely made solid substance
🇬🇧 **2.** such a drug or preparation packaged in a certain quantity, which is soluble in water

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kahtlane valge pulber osutus surmavaks mürkaineks ritsiiniks. ||The suspicious white powder turned out to be the deadly poison ricin.||
● Pulbriks jahvatatud rosmariiniokkad. ||Rosemary sprigs ground into powder.||
● Kaneelipulber. ||Cinnamon powder.||
● Kriidipulber. ||Chalk powder.||
● Kilekotis oli kahtlane valge pulber. ||The plastic bag contained a suspicious white powder.||
● Lastele on probiootikume saada ka pulbrina. ||Probiotics for children are also available in powder form.||
