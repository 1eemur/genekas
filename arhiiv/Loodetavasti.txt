**Tänane WOTD**
Loodetavasti - Määrsõna/Adverb
*loodetavasti*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 nagu tahaks loota, nagu võib loota 
🇬🇧 hopefully, as one would hope, as one might hope 

**Näited/Examples:**
● Loodetavasti nõelravi aitab sind. ||Hopefully acupuncture will help you.||
● Loodetavasti järgmist korda ei tule. ||Hopefully there won't be a next time.||
● Loodetavasti leiab May ruttu vastuseid. ||Hopefully May will quickly find some answers.||
● Loodetavasti saad sa aru, et ma lähen sellega linnapea juurde. ||Hopefully you understand that I'm going to the mayor with this.||
● Loodetavasti ta leiab selle, mida iganes ta otsib, ja siis ma saan selle ära võtta. ||Hopefully he'll find whatever he's looking for, and then I can take it.||
