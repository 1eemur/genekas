Sisustus - Nimisõna/Noun
*sisustus, sisustuse, sisustust; sisustused, sisustuste, sisustusi*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** korteri vm ruumi mööbel, seadmed vm sisseseade,mööbel, seadmed, vaibad, kardinad vms toas või majas
🇬🇧 **1.** furniture, equipment, etc. of an apartment or room, furniture, equipment, carpets, curtains, etc. in a room or house

**Näited/Examples:**
● Baari sisustus lõhuti. ||The bar’s interior was destroyed.||
● Paul eelistas moodsat sisustust. ||Paul preferred modern furnishings.||
● Korteris on moodsa sisustusega köök. ||The apartment has a modern kitchen.||
● Sisustus kuulub kahtlusteta ajastu stiili. ||The interior is unmistakably in the style of the era.||
● Korter ning selle sisustus ei olnud kindlustatud. ||The apartment and its furnishings were not insured.||
