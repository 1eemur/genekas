**Tänane WOTD**
Huvi - Nimisõna/Noun
*huvi, huvi, huvi; huvid, huvide, huve/huvisid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** (keskendunud, kirglik) soov, tahtmine midagi teada saada või tundma õppida; 
🇪🇪 **2.** tahtmine, soov midagi teha
🇪🇪 **3.** see, mida keegi (äris, poliitikas vm) taotleb, hrl kasu vm soodsad asjaolud
🇬🇧 **1.** (concentrated, passionate) interest, wanting to know or get to know something
🇬🇧 **2.** wanting, wishing to do something
🇬🇧 **3.** what someone (in business, politics, etc.) requests, usually benefits, etc. favorable circumstances

**Näited/Examples:**
● Olen Idamaade filosoofia vastu kogu aeg huvi tundnud. ||I have always been interested in Eastern philosophy.||
● Ajaloohuvi. ||Interest in history.||
● Spordihuvi. ||Sports interest.||
● Tema huvi on kirikutüli lahendada. ||His interest is to settle the church dispute.||
● Selline seadus rikuks valijate huve. ||Such a law would violate the interests of voters.||
● Mul on omad huvid ka kaubanduses. ||I also have my own interests in trade.||
● Finantshuvid. ||Financial interests.||
