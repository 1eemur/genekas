**Tänane WOTD**
Eriomane - Omadussõna/Adjective
*eriomane, eriomase, eriomast; eriomased, eriomaste, eriomaseid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** millelegi olemuslikult, tunnuslikult omane,millelegi või kellelegi eriti omane
🇬🇧 **1.** intrinsically, characteristically characteristic of something, especially characteristic of something or someone

**Näited/Examples:**
● Eriomased tunnused. ||Special features.||
● Voltaire'ile eriomane teravmeelsus. ||Voltaire's special wit.||
● Peipsimaa eriomane kultuur. ||The special culture of Peips County.||
● Sedalaadi otsing on inimesele eriomane. ||This type of search is specific to humans.||
● Põllumajandustootjad saavad toetust vastavalt eriomastele piirangutele. ||Farmers receive support in accordance with specific restrictions.||
