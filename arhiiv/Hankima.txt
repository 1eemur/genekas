**Tänane WOTD**
Hankima - Tegusõna/Verb
*hankima, hankida; hangib, hankis; hankinud, hangitud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** midagi kuskilt või kelleltki endale saama, enda tarbeks soetama, hrl selleks pingutades, vaeva nähes, otsides või asju ajades midagi saama
🇬🇧 **1.** to get something from somewhere or someone, to buy for your own use, usually struggling, to get something while looking or doing something

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kust selleks raha hankida? ||Where to get the money for it?||
● Sa peaksid endale tööd hankima. ||You should get a job for yourself.||
● Õnnestus lõunasöögiks kala hankida. ||He managed to get fish for lunch.||
● Koolile hangiti uued arvutid. ||New computers were procured for the school.||
● Materjali tuli hankida ka naabertehastest. ||The material had to be obtained from neighboring factories.||
● Piirivalve peab hakkama uuesti lennukeid ja koptereid hankima. ||The border guards need to start buying planes and helicopters again.||
