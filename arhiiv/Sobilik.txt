Sobilik - Omadussõna/Adjective
*sobilik, sobiliku, sobilikku; sobilikud, sobilike,sobilikkude, sobilikke/sobilikkusid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** millekski (hästi) kõlbav, teatud tingimustele vastav,millegagi kooskõlas olev, ladusalt toimiv,selline, mis sobib, on hea ja õige
🇬🇧 **1.**  suitable , fit , right, to something (well) sounding, conforming to certain conditions, in line with something, smoothly functional, one that fits is good and right

**Näited/Examples:**
● Mida teha, kui nõuanne ei tundu sobilik? ||What should you do if the advice does not seem appropriate?||
● Peaksid kandma meiki, mis on sinu naha jaoks sobilik. ||You should wear makeup that suits your skin type.||
● Kartulile on sobilik ka soe ja piisavalt kobe muld. ||Potatoes also thrive in warm and sufficiently moist soil.||
● Selline osalejate arv on sobilik, et inimeste vahel tekiks arutelu. ||Such a number of participants is suitable for discussion between people.||
● Iga patsiendiga tegeletakse individuaalselt ja leitakse just tema jaoks sobilik programm. ||Each patient is treated individually and a program suitable for them is found.||
