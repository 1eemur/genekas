Tagapool - Määrsõna/Adverb & Eessõna/Preposition
*tagapool*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** (eessõna) (kaugemal) taga, (millegi või kellegi suhtes) kaugemal,(kaugemal) taga,tagumises osas
🇪🇪 **2.** hiljem, allpool (nt järgnevas tekstis)
🇪🇪 **3.** (paremusjärjestuses) lõpu pool, saavutustelt, paremuselt teistest kehvem
🇬🇧 **1.** (preposition) (farther) behind, (relative to something or someone) further, (farther) behind, in the back
🇬🇧 **2.** later, below (eg in the following text)
🇬🇧 **3.** (in order of merit) towards the end, in terms of achievements, inferior to others

**Näited/Examples:**
● Tagapool istujad ei näinud midagi. ||Those sitting at the back couldn't see anything.||
● Seisnuks ma meeter tagapool, oleks palliga vastu pead saanud. ||If I had been standing a meter behind, I would have been hit in the head by the ball.||
● Mootor asub tagapool esisilda. ||The engine is located behind the front axle.||
● Vend seisis õest veidi tagapool. ||The brother stood a little behind his sister.||
● Ta on edetabelis kuskil tagapool. ||He is somewhere near the bottom of the rankings.||
