Baka - Nimisõna/Noun
*baka, baka, bakat; bakad, bakade, bakasid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** kõrghariduse madalaima astme õpe, mille kestel üliõpilane omandab eriala alusteadmised ja -oskused
🇪🇪 **2.** inimene, kes on lõpetanud hrl kolmeaastase õppekava ja saanud bakalaureusekraadi; 
🇬🇧 **1.** bachelor's degree, study at the lowest level of higher education, during which the student acquires the basic knowledge and skills of the specialty
🇬🇧 **2.** a person who has completed a three-year study program and received a bachelor's degree

**Näited/Examples:**
● Lõpetasin TLÜ-s baka ja TÜ-s magistri. ||I finished my bachelor's degree at TLÜ and my master's degree at UT.||
● Palju õnne baka puhul! ||Congratulations on the bachelor's degree!||
● Ei tahaks bakat viimasel kursusel pooleli jätta. ||I wouldn't want to leave the baccalaureate unfinished in the last year.||
● Ma jagasin baka ajal korterit sõbraga. ||I shared an apartment with a friend during my undergrad.||
● Kas sa oled baka? ||Are you an undergrad student?||
