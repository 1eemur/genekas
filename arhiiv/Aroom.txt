**Tänane WOTD**
Aroom - Nimisõna/Noun
*aroom, aroomi, aroomi; aroomid, aroomide, aroome/aroomisid*
*Lühike Sisseütlev: aroomi*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 (meeldiv) lõhn  
🇬🇧 aroma, a (pleasant) smell

**Näited/Examples:**
● Mina eelistan mehelikke aroome. ||I prefer masculine aromas.||
● Veini aroom on kergelt vürtsikas. ||The wine's aroma is slightly spicy.||
● Õhus heljus kohvi ja konjaki aroom. ||The aroma of coffee and cognac wafted in the air.||
● Tee oli puuvilja aroomi ja ananassi maitsega. ||The tea had a fruity aroma and a pineapple flavour.||
● Aroom on peen ja hõrk, meeldiva puuviljavarjundiga. ||The aroma is fine and delicate with pleasant hints of fruit.||
