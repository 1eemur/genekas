**Tänane WOTD**
Surnuaed - Nimisõna/Noun
*surnuaed, surnuaia, surnuaeda; surnuaiad, surnuaedade, surnuaedu/surnuaedasid*
*Lühike Sisseütlev: surnuaeda*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 maa-ala surnute matmiseks  
🇬🇧 cemetery, land for the burial of the dead

**Näited/Examples:**
● Surnuaias on kaks nimetut hauda. ||There are two unmarked graves in the cemetery.||
● Lahkunu maeti kiriku surnuaeda. ||The deceased was buried in the church cemetery.||
● Surnuaia väravas seisis jahimees püssiga. ||A hunter with a gun stood at the cemetery gate.||
● Langenud sõdurid maeti Rahumäe surnuaeda. ||The fallen soldiers were buried in the Rahumäe cemetery.||
● Mulle meeldib käia vanades surnuaedades sammaldunud raudriste vaatamas. ||I like to visit old cemeteries to see the moss-covered iron crosses.||
