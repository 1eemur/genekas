**Tänane WOTD**
Spetsialist - Nimisõna/Noun
*spetsialist, spetsialisti, spetsialisti; spetsialistid, spetsialistide, spetsialistisid/spetsialiste*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** inimene, kes tunneb hästi oma ala, hrl vastava haridusega inimene; 
🇬🇧 **1.** a person who knows his field well, i.e. a person with relevant education

**Näited/Examples:**
● IT-spetsialist. ||IT specialist.||
● Vanemspetsialist. ||Senior specialist.||
● Tuuleenergeetika spetsialist. ||Wind energy specialist.||
● Ilma töökogemuseta noort spetsialisti ei kipu keegi tööle võtma. ||No one is likely to hire a young specialist without work experience.||
● Insener-tehniline spetsialist seadistab arvuti võrgu jaoks. ||An engineer-technical specialist sets up the computer for the network.||
