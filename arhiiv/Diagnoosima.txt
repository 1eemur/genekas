**Tänane WOTD**
Diagnoosima - Tegusõna/Verb
*diagnoosima, diagnoosida; diagnoosin, diagnoosisin; diagnoosinud, diagnoositud*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 haiguse olemust ja iseloomu määrama, diagnoosi panema 
🇬🇧 to diagnose, to determine the nature and character of the disease, make a diagnosis

**Näited/Examples:**
● Vanaemal diagnoositi luude hõrenemine. ||My grandmother was diagnosed with osteoporosis.||
● Psühhiaater diagnoosis depressiooni ja ka ärevushäire. ||The psychiatrist diagnosed depression and also anxiety disorder.||
● Autolt löögi saanud poisil diagnoositi haiglas peapõrutus. ||A boy hit by a car was diagnosed with a concussion at the hospital.||
● Ravi edukus oleneb sellest, kui kiiresti haigus diagnoositakse ning raviga alustatakse. ||The success of treatment depends on how quickly the disease is diagnosed and treatment is started.||
● Kliinilise auditi eesmärk oli uurida, kuidas Eestis depressiooni diagnoositakse ning ravitakse. ||The purpose of the clinical audit was to investigate how depression is diagnosed and treated in Estonia.||
