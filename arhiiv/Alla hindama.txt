**Tänane WOTD**
Alla hindama - Tegusõna/Verb
*Alla hindama, Alla hinnata; Hindab alla, Hindas alla; Alla hinnanud, Alla hinnatud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** hinda alandama, hinda odavamaks tegema
🇪🇪 **2.** alahindama, tegelikust vähem tähtsaks pidama
🇬🇧 **1.** to lower the price, make the price cheaper
🇬🇧 **2.** to underestimate, to consider less important than the real thing

**Näited/Examples:**
● Vananenud kaupa tuleb kõvasti alla hinnata. ||Obsolete goods must be greatly discounted.||
● Paljudes poodides hinnatakse alla pagaritooted õhtul kaks tundi enne poe sulgemist. ||In many stores, bakery products are discounted in the evening two hours before the store closes.||
● Norman Optika poodides on kuni poole võrra alla hinnatud päikeseprillid. ||Sunglasses are up to half off in Norman Optika stores.||
● Kaupluses on suur osa jalanõusid alla hinnatud. ||In the shop, much of the footwear is discounted.||
● Ma ei taha Valgevene koondise taset alla hinnata. ||I don't want to underestimate the level of the Belarusian national team.||
