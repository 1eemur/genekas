Tolleaegne - Omadussõna/Adjective
*tolleaegne, tolleaegse, tolleaegset; tolleaegsed, tolleaegsete, tolleaegseid*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 mingil möödunud ajal, tol ajal olnud, toimunud vms 
🇬🇧 then, at that time, having been or occurred at some time in the past

**Näited/Examples:**
● „Minu tolleaegne sõbratar tundis samuti seda inimest, kes mind õpetas,” vastas Paulo. ||My girlfriend back in those days also knew the person who was teaching me, answered Paulo.||
● Ühel lõunavaheajal soovis aga minuga rääkida vend Joseph Rutherford, tolleaegne piiblilise haridustöö ülevaataja. ||During one lunch break, however, Brother Joseph Rutherford, who had oversight of the work at the time, asked to speak with me.||
● Tolleaegsed vastuvõetavad standardid ei ole võrreldavad nendega, mis on täna teostatavad ja poliitiliselt oodatud. ||The standard that was acceptable at that time is not comparable with what is feasible and politically expected today.||
● Suuremaks kasvades õppis ta Seadust ühe tolleaegse tuntuima „professori” Gamaalieli juures. ||When he grew older, he studied the Law with one of the foremost “professors” of the time —Gamaliel.||

**Sünonüümid/Synonyms:** 
tollane, tookordne, toonane
