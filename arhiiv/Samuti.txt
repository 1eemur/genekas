**Tänane WOTD**
Samuti - Määrsõna/Adverb
*samuti*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** millegagi võrreldes samamoodi, ühesuguselt, nagu keegi teine või miski muu 
🇪🇪  **1.1** kellelegi või millelegi lisaks, peale selle 
🇬🇧 **1.** in the same way compared to something else, in the same way as someone else or something else
🇬🇧 **1.1** in addition to anyone or something else, also

**Näited/Examples:**
● Tema poeg Juha on samuti tuntud poksija. ||His son Juha is also a well-known boxer.||
● Samuti oli midagi toimunud ta peas. ||Something had also happened inside his head.||
● Pingeline olukord Ida-Tšaadis ei ole samuti palju aidanud. ||The tense situation in eastern Chad has also not helped much.||
● Peame lihtsustama tulevikku, kuid samuti tegema rahu minevikuga. ||We must simplify the future, but also make peace with the past.||
● Vahistamise hetkel mehel relva polnud, samuti ei osutanud ta politseile vastupanu. ||At the time of the arrest, the man had no weapon, he also did not resist the police.||
