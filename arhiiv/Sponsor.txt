Sponsor - Nimisõna/Noun
*sponsor, sponsori, sponsorit; sponsorid, sponsorite, sponsoreid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** kellegi või millegi (nt ürituse, projekti, sportlase) rahaline toetaja,millegi või kellegi rahaline toetaja
🇬🇧 **1.** sponsor, a financial supporter of someone or something (eg an event, project, athlete), something or someone's financial supporter

**Näited/Examples:**
● Tiimil on rikas sponsor. ||The team has a rich sponsor.||
● Otsime uut sponsorit. ||We are looking for a new sponsor.||
● Noor suusataja otsib sponsoreid. ||The young skier is looking for sponsors.||
● Meie firma on selle ürituse ametlik sponsor. ||Our company is the official sponsor of this event.||
● Firma on olümpiakomitee ametlik sponsor. ||The company is the official sponsor of the Olympic Committee.||
