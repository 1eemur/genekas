**Tänane WOTD**
Asuma - Tegusõna/Verb
*asuma, asuda; asub, asus; asunud, asutud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** kusagil, mingis asukohas paiknema või olema,kuskil olema, paiknema
🇪🇪 **2.** (inimese, muu elusolendi kohta:) kuskil elama või elutsema, (püsivalt) mingis kohas viibima
🇪🇪 **3.** mingit tegevust alustama, midagi tegema hakkama,end sisse seadma (nt mingisse asendisse, toimingusse),mingit tegevust alustama
🇪🇪 **4.** kuhugi elama minema või tulema
🇬🇧 **1.** to be or be somewhere, in some location, to be somewhere, to be located
🇬🇧 **2.** (about a person, other living creature) to live somewhere, to stay (permanently) in a place
🇬🇧 **3.** to start some activity, to start doing something, to settle down (e.g. in some position, action), to start some activity
🇬🇧 **4.** to go or come to live somewhere

**Näited/Examples:**
● Hotell asub veinikeldriga samas majas. ||The hotel is located in the same building as the wine cellar.||
● Vanaema korter asus jaamahoone teisel korrusel. ||Grandma's apartment was on the second floor of the station building.||
● Maavärina epitsenter asus Vaikse ookeani põhjas. ||The epicenter of the earthquake was located at the bottom of the Pacific Ocean.||
● Meeskond asub hetkel meistriliiga tabelis kolmandal kohal. ||The team is currently in third place in the championship table.||
● Kus see asub? ||Where is it located?||
● Asume aadressil Punane 16. ||We are located at Punane 16.||
● Maja asub järve ääres. ||The house is located by the lake.||
● Kirjanik asus aastaid välismaal. ||The writer was abroad for many years.||
● Joonatan asus rooli. ||Jonathan took the wheel.||
● Hommikul asusime teele. ||In the morning we set off.||
