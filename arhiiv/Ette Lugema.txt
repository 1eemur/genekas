**Tänane WOTD**
Ette Lugema - Tegusõna/Verb
*ette lugema, ette lugeda; loen ette, lugesin ette; ette lugenud, ette loetud*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** midagi häälega, valjusti lugedes kuulaja(i)le esitama 
🇪🇪 **2.** midagi nimepidi üles lugema, ühekaupa välja ütlema 
🇬🇧 **1.** to say something aloud, reading aloud to the listener(s) 
🇬🇧 **2.** to recite something by name, to say something one word at a time

**Näited/Examples:**
● Lugesin lapsele õhtul raamatut ette. ||I read a book aloud to my child in the evening.||
● Linnapea luges ette iseseisvusmanifesti. ||The mayor read out the independence manifesto.||
● Konsensuse alusel otsustati kiri ette lugeda. ||By consensus it was decided to read out the letter.||
● Mind pandi alati klassi ette lugema, kuna lugesin õpetajate arvates selgelt ja ilmekalt. ||I was always made to read aloud to the class because the teachers thought I read clearly and eloquently.||
● Siiri loeb ette maad, kuhu reisi käigus tahaks minna. ||Siiri reads out the countries she would like to visit on the trip.||
