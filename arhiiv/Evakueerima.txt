**Tänane WOTD**
Evakueerima - Tegusõna/Verb
*Evakueerima, Evakueerida; Evakueerib, Evakueeris; Evakueerinud, Evakueeritud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** kedagi või midagi ohupiirkonnast organiseeritult ära viima (nt sõja ajal, loodusõnnetuse puhul)
🇬🇧 **1.** to evacuate, to remove someone or something from the danger area in an organized manner (e.g. during war, in the case of a natural disaster)

**Näited/Examples:**
● Lennujaamas olnud inimesed evakueeriti. ||People at the airport were evacuated.||
● Turistid evakueeriti Bangkoki lennujaamast hotellidesse. ||Tourists were evacuated from Bangkok airport to hotels.||
● Politsei evakueeris kaubamajast kõik ostjad ja töötajad. ||The police evacuated all shoppers and staff from the department store.||
● Inimesed evakueeriti hoonest, keegi tulekahjus kannatada ei saanud. ||People were evacuated from the building, nobody was injured in the fire.||
● Süttinud reisilaeva 400 reisijat evakueeriti kiiresti. ||The 400 passengers on board the passenger ship that had caught fire were quickly evacuated.||
