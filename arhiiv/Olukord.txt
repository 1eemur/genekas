**Tänane WOTD**
Olukord - Nimisõna/Noun
*olukord, olukorra, olukorda; olukorrad, olukordade, olukordi/olukordasid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** asjade üldine olek, seis teatud ajahetkel,mingi sündmuse asjaolud, eluga seotud tingimused
🇬🇧 **1.** situation, the general state of things, the situation at a certain point in time, the circumstances of an event, conditions related to life

**Näited/Examples:**
● Olukord muutus kümme aastat tagasi. ||The situation changed ten years ago.||
● Igatahes tuli olukorrale varsti lahendus leida. ||Anyway, a solution to the situation had to be found soon.||
● Majandusolukord. ||Economic situation.||
● Hetkeolukord. ||Current situation.||
● Ohuolukord. ||Unsafe condition.||
● Tavaolukord. ||Normal situation.||
● Sattusin keerulisse olukorda. ||I got into a difficult situation.||
● Pere majanduslik olukord on halb. ||The economic situation of the family is bad.||
