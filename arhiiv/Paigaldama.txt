Paigaldama - Tegusõna/Verb
*Paigaldama, Paigaldada; Paigaldab, Paigaldas; Paigaldanud, Paigaldatud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** oma ettenähtud kohale (töökorda) seadma, paigale panema (hrl esemeid, seadmeid, tarkvara)
🇪🇪 **2.** (luumurru vms vigastuse korral:) paigast nihkunud luuotsi õigesse asendisse panema; 
🇬🇧 **1.** to put in place (working order), put in place (usually objects, devices, software)
🇬🇧 **2.** (in case of a fracture or other injury) to put the displaced bone ends in the correct position

**Näited/Examples:**
● Ristmikule paigaldati uus foor. ||A new traffic light was installed at the intersection.||
● Kadrioru parki paigaldatakse uued valgustid. ||New lights will be installed in Kadriorg Park.||
● Töömehed paigaldavad kiriku aknale vitraaži. ||Workers install stained glass on the church's window.||
● Majja paigaldatakse tõhusam tuletõrjesüsteem. ||A more efficient fire protection system will be installed in the house.||
● Kaamerad paigaldatakse koolimaja koridoridesse ja fuajeedesse. ||Cameras will be installed in the corridors and lobbies of the school building.||
