**Tänane WOTD**
Kosmonaut - Nimisõna/Noun
*kosmonaut, kosmonaudi, kosmonauti; kosmonaudid, kosmonautide, kosmonaute/kosmonautisid*
*Lühike Sisseütlev: kosmonauti*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 kosmosesõiduki meeskonna liige (hrl Venemaal ja endises Nõukogude Liidus) 
🇬🇧 cosmonaut, aerospace crew member (usually in Russia and the former Soviet Union) 

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kosmonaudi naisel peab olema tugev iseloom. ||A cosmonaut's wife must have a strong character.||
● Kosmoselaeva pardal oli kaks kosmonauti. ||There were two cosmonauts on board the spacecraft.||
● Esimesi kosmonaute ja astronaute jumaldas kogu maailm. ||The first cosmonauts and astronauts were adored by the whole world.||
● Kosmonautide vastumeelsus märkmete avaldamise vastu on arusaadav. ||The cosmonauts' reluctance to publish their notes is understandable.||
● Toreda üllatusena nägin kosmonautidele mõeldud pakendatud šokolaaditükkidega jäätist. ||As a nice surprise, I saw ice-cream packaged for cosmonauts with chocolate chips.||
