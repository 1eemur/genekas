**Tänane WOTD**
Külaline - Nimisõna/Noun
*külaline, külalise, külalist; külalised, külaliste, külalisi*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** inimene, kes on kellegi juures külas või võtab võõrana osa mingist peost vm üritusest,inimene, kes on kutsutud kellelegi külla, peole vm üritusele
🇪🇪 **2.** hotellis ööbiv, restoranis olev vms inimene teda teenindava asutuse suhtes
🇬🇧 **1.** guest, a person who is visiting someone or attending a party or other event as a guest, a person who is invited to someone's house, party or other event
🇬🇧 **2.** a person staying in a hotel, restaurant or similar establishment in relation to the establishment serving him or her

**Näited/Examples:**
● Sünnipäevale tuleb palju külalisi. ||There are many guests to the birthday.||
● Kutsusin laupäevaks paar külalist. ||I invited a couple of guests for Saturday.||
● Konverentsi külalised paluti õhtusöögile. ||Conference guests were invited to dinner.||
● Muuseumi direktor tutvustas külalistele lahkelt Eesti spordiajalugu. ||The director of the museum kindly introduced the guests to the history of Estonian sport.||
● Hotelli külalised kurtsid, et kraanivesi on kummalist värvi ja haiseb. ||Guests at the hotel complained that the tap water was a strange color and smelled.||
