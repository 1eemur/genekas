**Tänane WOTD**
Orav - Nimisõna/Noun
*orav, orava, oravat; oravad, oravate, oravaid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** metsas (ja parkides) puudel elav pika koheva sabaga väike loom (peamiselt põhjapoolkeral)
🇪🇪 **2.** oravalt saadud karusnahk
🇪🇪 **3.** (reformierakondlase kohta)
🇬🇧 **1.** squirrel, a small animal with a long fluffy tail living on trees in forests (and parks) (mainly in the northern hemisphere)
🇬🇧 **2.** fur from a squirrel
🇬🇧 **3.** (about a reformierakond member)

**Näited/Examples:**
● Orav sööb pähkleid. ||The squirrel eats nuts.||
● Orav kogub talvevarusid, mida peidab samblasse. ||A squirrel collects winter supplies to hide in the moss.||
● Nägime mitut oravat korraga mööda puutüve edasi-tagasi jooksmas ja mängimas. ||We saw several squirrels at the same time running back and forth along the tree trunk and playing.||
● Oravast krae. ||Squirrel collar.||
● Vaatame, kui palju oravad on valmis kompromisse tegema. ||Let's see how much the squirrels are willing to compromise.||
