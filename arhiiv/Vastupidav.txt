**Tänane WOTD**
Vastupidav - Omadussõna/Adjective
*vastupidav, vastupidava, vastupidavat; vastupidavad, vastupidavate, vastupidavaid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** (esemete, materjalide vm kohta:) mingi mõju suhtes püsiv, selline, mis kaua heas seisundis püsib ja ei lagune, tugev või kindel
🇪🇪 **2.** (organismi või elusolendi, tema omaduste kohta:) hea vastupanuvõimega, visa, sitke või iseloomult tugev
🇬🇧 **1.** (of objects, materials, etc.) durable, stable with respect to some effect, one that remains in good condition for a long time and does not decay, strong or firm
🇬🇧 **2.** (of an organism or living being, its characteristics) tough, with good resistance, tenacious, tough or strong in character

**Näited/Examples:**
● Mulle meeldivad teksad, nad on lihtsad, vastupidavad, mugavad. ||I like jeans, they are simple, durable, comfortable.||
● Jaapanis osatakse ehitada tugevatele maavärinatele vastupidavaid maju. ||In Japan, they know how to build houses resistant to strong earthquakes.||
● Tuberkuloosipisik on üsna vastupidav. ||The tuberculosis germ is quite resistant.||
● Kiire kasvu korral on taim haigustele ja kahjuritele vähem vastupidav. ||With rapid growth, the plant is less resistant to diseases and pests.||
● Peab olema vastupidav, et taluda elu kloostri müüride vahel. ||One must be hardy to endure life within the walls of a monastery.||
