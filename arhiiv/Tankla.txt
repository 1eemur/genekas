**Tänane WOTD**
Tankla - Nimisõna/Noun
*tankla, tankla, tanklat; tanklad, tanklate, tanklaid*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 autode jt sõidukite kütusega tankimiseks mõeldud koht koos vastava varustusega  
🇬🇧 gas station, a place for the refuelling of cars and other vehicles with the appropriate equipment

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kus see tankla asus? ||Where was this gas station located?||
● Siin ei ole tanklat miilide kaugusel. ||There's no gas station for miles around here.||
● Kas oskate öelda, kus siin tankla asub? ||Can you tell me where there's a gas station around here?||
● Me oleksime pidanud temalt küsima, kas siin mõnda teist tanklat pole. ||We should've asked him if there's another gas station around.||
