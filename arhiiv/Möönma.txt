**Tänane WOTD**
Möönma - Tegusõna/Verb
*möönma, möönda; möönan, möönsin;  möönnud, mööndud*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 (justkui vastu tahtmist) tunnistama, (järele andes) 
🇬🇧 (as if against one's will) to admit, (while yielding) to accept 

**Näited/Examples:**
● Tuleb möönda, et see pole alati kerge. ||Admittedly, this is not always easy.||
● Tehtud vigu möönis ka valitsusjuht. ||The mistakes made were also admitted by the head of government.||
● Noormees möönis, et ta teenib väga head palka. ||The young man admitted that he earns a very good salary.||
● Ma pean möönma, et see on veel viletsam kui ma ootasin. ||I have to admit that it's even worse than I expected.||
● Enamik abieluinimesi möönab, et nende abielus on tulnud ette raskusi. ||Most married people admit that they have experienced difficulties in their marriage.||
