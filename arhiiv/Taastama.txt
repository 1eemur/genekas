Taastama - Tegusõna/Verb
*Taastama, Taastada; Taastab, Taastas; Taastanud, Taastatud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** endist kuju andma, endisesse seisukorda seadma; 
🇬🇧 **1.** to restore, to give the former shape, to put in the former condition

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kuidas taastada investorite usaldust? ||How to restore investor confidence?||
● Kohus taastab omaniku õigused. ||The court restores the rights of the owner.||
● Une ajal organism taastab ennast. ||During sleep, the organism restores itself.||
● Hoone taastati sõjaeelsel kujul. ||The building was restored to its pre-war form.||
● Toomkirik taastati muinsuskaitsjate eestvõttel. ||The cathedral was restored with the help of heritage conservationists.||

**Sünonüümid/Synonyms:** 
taaslooma, taastekitama
