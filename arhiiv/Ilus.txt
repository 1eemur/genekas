**Tänane WOTD**
Ilus - Omadussõna/Adjective
*ilus, ilusa, ilusat; ilusad, ilusate, ilusaid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** välimuselt meeldiv, vaadates rahuldust pakkuv, väga kena,väga kena
🇪🇪 **2.** muljet avaldav, väga meelepärane või rikkalik, kellegi soovidele vastav
🇪🇪 **3.** rõõmu valmistav, hingele hea, meeltülendav vms
🇪🇪 **4.** kiiduväärt, viisakas, kombekohane,hea, viisakas
🇪🇪 **5.** osutab irooniliselt, et asi pole tegelikult üldse hea või tore, vaid hoopis vastupidi
🇬🇧 **1.** beautiful, pleasant in appearance, satisfying to look at
🇬🇧 **2.** impressive, very pleasing or rich, meeting one's wishes
🇬🇧 **3.** joyful, good for the soul, satisfying, etc
🇬🇧 **4.** commendable, polite, proper, good
🇬🇧 **5.** ironically indicates that it is not actually good or nice at all, but quite the opposite

**Näited/Examples:**
● Ta on tark ja ilus naine. ||She is a smart and beautiful woman.||
● Ilmajaam lubab ilusat ilma. ||The weather station promises beautiful weather.||
● Temast on kõikjal palju ilusaid pilte, aga intervjuusid mõni üksik. ||There are many beautiful pictures of him everywhere, but only a few interviews.||
● Ta on kõige ilusam naine, keda ma näinud olen. ||She is the most beautiful woman I have ever seen.||
● Aknast avanes ilus vaade. ||A beautiful view opened from the window.||
● Täna on ilus ilm. ||The weather is beautiful today.||
● Kolme minuti jooksul löödi kaks ilusat väravat. ||Two beautiful goals were scored within three minutes.||
● Enne valimisi antakse ikka ilusaid lubadusi. ||Beautiful promises are still given before the elections.||
● Aitäh ilusate sõnade eest. ||Thank you for the nice words.||
● Ilusad mälestused. ||Beautiful memories.||
