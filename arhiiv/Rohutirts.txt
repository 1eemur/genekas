Rohutirts - Nimisõna/Noun
*rohutirts, rohutirtsu, rohutirtsu; rohutirtsud, rohutirtsude, rohutirtse/rohutirtsusid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** siristav hüppejalgadega putukas
🇪🇪 **2.** (toiduna)
🇬🇧 **1.** grasshopper, a chirping insect with jumping legs
🇬🇧 **2.** (as food)

**Näited/Examples:**
● Siinsamas lähedal siristas rohutirts. ||A grasshopper chirped nearby.||
● Kas luuletaja pole nagu muusikat tegev rohutirts? ||Isn't a poet like a grasshopper making music?||
● Elas nagu rohutirts igast päevast rõõmu tundes. ||He lived like a grasshopper, enjoying every day.||
● Oranži samblaga kivil ronib roheline rohutirts. ||A green grasshopper climbs on a rock with orange moss.||
● Röstitud rohutirtsud. ||Roasted grasshoppers.||
