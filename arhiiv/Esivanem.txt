Esivanem - Nimisõna/Noun
*esivanem, esivanema, esivanemat; esivanemad, esivanemate, esivanemaid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** kellegi isa- või emapoolne eellane (vanaisa, vanavanaema vm),inimese (või looma) kaugem eellane
🇬🇧 **1.** someone's paternal or maternal ancestor (grandfather, great-grandmother, etc.), distant ancestor of a person (or animal)

**Näited/Examples:**
● Minu esivanemad on etnilised soomlased. ||My ancestors are ethnic Finns.||
● Austame esivanemaid , kes kunagi alla ei andnud. ||We honor our ancestors, who never gave up.||
● Rahvas on öelnud lahti oma esivanemate usust. ||The people have turned their backs on the faith of their ancestors.||
● Koerte esivanemateks arvatakse olevat hundid ja šaakalid. ||Wolves and jackals are believed to be the ancestors of dogs.||
● Inimese kauged esivanemad tegelesid küttimise ja kalastamisega. ||The distant ancestors of man were engaged in hunting and fishing.||
