**Tänane WOTD**
Austama - Tegusõna/Verb
*austama, austada; austan, austasin; austatud, austanud*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** kellessegi sügava austuse, tunnustava lugupidamise suhtuma 
🇪🇪 **1.1** millessegi aukartusega suhtuma, midagi tingimusteta tunnustama 
🇪🇪 **1.2** kedagi oma hinges armastama 
🇪🇪 **2.** kellelegi lugupidamist, austust avaldama, au osutama 
🇬🇧 **1.** to have a deep appreciative respect for someone, to honor
🇬🇧 **1.1** to respect, to treat something with reverence, to acknowledge something unconditionally
🇬🇧 **1.2** to love someone in your soul 
🇬🇧 **2.** to pay respects to someone, to pay homage to someone

**Näited/Examples:**
● Armastan ja austan oma naist. ||I love and respect my wife.||
● Normaalne inimene austab riigi seadusi. ||A normal person respects national laws.||
● Imetlen ja austan sinu tarkust. ||I admire and respect your wisdom.||
● Emakest loodust peab austama. ||You got to respect mother nature.||
● Tema mälestust austati minutilise leinaseisakuga. ||His memory was honored with a minute's silence.||
● Ma tõstatan selle küsimuse pisut laiemalt kui seda tegi austatud parlamendiliige, kuid ma teen seda tahtlikult. ||I am putting it slightly more broadly than the honourable Member did, but very deliberately so.||
