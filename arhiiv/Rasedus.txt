Rasedus - Nimisõna/Noun
*rasedus, raseduse, rasedust; rasedused, raseduste, rasedusi*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** naise füsioloogiline seisund, mille ajal tema organismis areneb viljastatud munarakust laps
🇬🇧 **1.** pregnancy, a woman's physiological condition during which her body develops a child from a fertilized egg

**Näited/Examples:**
● Rasedus on kulgenud normaalselt. ||The pregnancy has gone normally.||
● Mina käisin kogu raseduse ajal regulaarselt isikliku massööri juures. ||I had a regular personal massage throughout my pregnancy.||
● Raseduse esimestel nädalatel ei saanud ma heeringast isu täis. ||I couldn't get enough of herring in the first few weeks of pregnancy.||
● Raseduse ajal vajad rohkem vitamiine ja toitaineid kui iial varem. ||You need more vitamins and nutrients during pregnancy than ever before.||
● Kui kahtlustad rasedust, saad seda testida apteegist ostetava rasedustesti abil.. ||If you suspect pregnancy, you can test for it with a pregnancy test you can buy from a pharmacy.||
