**Tänane WOTD**
Pidevalt - Määrsõna/Adverb
*pidevalt*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 kogu aeg, ilma vahet pidamata; väga tihti  
🇬🇧 constantly, continuously, continually; very often

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kahjuks toimub loodusõnnetusi pidevalt. ||Unfortunately, disasters will continue to happen.||
● Pidevalt puhus tugev tuul, lennutades liiva, mis torkis nahka ja oli silmadele valus. ||A strong wind blew continually, blowing sand that stung the skin and hurt the eyes.||
● Su keharakud on pidevalt voolu all. ||Your cells are constantly in a state of flux.||
● Loodus muutub pidevalt. ||Nature is constantly changing.||
