**Tänane WOTD**
Alati - Määrsõna/Adverb
*alati*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** kogu aeg, igal ajal  
🇪🇪 **1.1** iga (viimane kui üks) kord 
🇬🇧 **1.** always, all the time, at any time
🇬🇧 **1.1** every (each and every) time 

**Näited/Examples:**
● Tähistaevas on alati ilus. ||The starry sky is always beautiful.||
● Asjad pole alati nii, nagu paistavad. ||Things aren't always what they seem.||
● Me oleme alati teadnud, et selline asi võib juhtuda. ||We have always known that such a thing could happen.||
● Kõrtsis on alati tore istuda. ||It's always nice to sit in a tavern.||
● Tema jutte ei saa alati uskuda. ||You can't always believe her stories.||
● Mitte alati, aga vahel küll. ||Not always, but sometimes.||
