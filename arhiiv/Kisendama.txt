Kisendama - Tegusõna/Verb
*kisendama, kisendada; kisendab, kisendas; kisendanud, kisendatud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** kõrge, terava häälega karjuma,sel viisil rääkima, hüüdma
🇪🇪 **2.** millestki ilmekalt, tungivalt märku andma, midagi hädasti, tungivalt vajama
🇬🇧 **1.** to make high, sharp voice scream, talk in this way, shout
🇬🇧 **2.** to signal something obviously, urgently, to need something urgently

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kajakad kisendavad. ||The seagulls cry.||
● Inimesed karjusid ja kisendasid. ||People were screaming and shouting.||
● Hing kisendab armastuse järele. ||The soul cries out of love.||
● Ülekohus kisendab. ||The injustice screams.||
● Mu mõtted kisendasid, suu aga vaikis. ||My thoughts screamed, but my mouth was silent.||
