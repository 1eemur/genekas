**Tänane WOTD**
Kokku panema - Tegusõna/Verb
*kokku panema, kokku panna; paneb kokku, pani kokku; pannud kokku, kokku pandud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** (teksti, kava, teost vms) koostama, (meeskonda vms) looma
🇪🇪 **2.** midagi ühte kohta või rühma koondama,ühendama, liitma
🇪🇪 **3.** (raha kohta:) millekski igaüks omalt poolt teatud summat andma, nii et kokku koguneb suurem summa
🇪🇪 **4.** detaile tervikuks liitma, neid omavahel sobitades, kinnitades vms moel
🇪🇪 **5.** midagi tihedamale, kompaktsemale, väiksemale kujule viima (kokku voltides, kokku rullides vm moel)
🇪🇪 **6.** kaasavõtmiseks kokku pakkima
🇪🇪 **7.** midagi üksteise vastu asetama
🇬🇧 **1.** (text, plan, work, etc.) to produce, (team, etc.) create
🇬🇧 **2.** to connect, merge something in one place or a group to concentrate
🇬🇧 **3.** (about money) something for their part to give a certain amount so that a larger amount accumulates in total
🇬🇧 **4.** to merge the details into a whole
🇬🇧 **5.** something denser, more compact, in a smaller shape (in total folds, together in rolls or otherwise)
🇬🇧 **6.** to bring along
🇬🇧 **7.** to place something against another thing

**Näited/Examples:**
● Maria aitas kõnet kokku panna. ||Maria helped put the speech together.||
● Järgmise raamatu tahan kokku panna oma luuletustest. ||The next book I want to put together from my poems.||
● Konkursile laekunud töödest pannakse kokku näitus. ||An exhibition will be put together from the works received.||
● Panime tiimi kokku. ||We put the team together.||
● Kuus valda pandi kokku. ||Six municipalities were put together.||
● Laps hakkas puslet kokku panema. ||The child began to put the puzzle together.||
