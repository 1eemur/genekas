**Tänane WOTD**
Muretsema - Tegusõna
*Muretsema, Muretseda; Muretseb, Muretses; Muretsenud, Muretsetud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** millegi pärast muret tundma, mingil põhjusel mures olema
🇪🇪 **2.** kellegi, millegi eest hoolitsema, hoolt kandma
🇪🇪 **3.** enesele, enda tarbeks hankima, (enesele) tekitama
🇬🇧 **1.** to worry about something, to be worried about something
🇬🇧 **2.** to take care of someone, something
🇬🇧 **3.** to obtain for one's own use, to cause (to oneself).

**Näited/Examples:**
● Ärge muretsege! ||Do not worry!||
● Muidugi hakkasid inimesed muretsema. ||Of course, people started to worry.||
● Ära muretse, kui jooned ei tule täiesti sirged. ||Don't worry if the lines don't come out perfectly straight.||
● Igaüks muretseb ainult enda eest. ||Everyone only cares about themselves.||
● Raha muretseti Soomest. ||The money was obtained from Finland.||
