**Tänane WOTD**
Probleem - Nimisõna/Noun
*probleem, probleemi, probleemi; probleemid, probleemide, probleeme/probleemisid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** midagi takistav, muret tekitav asjaolu või olukord, mis vajab lahendust ja millega tuleb tegelda,keeruline, raske asi,keeruline küsimus, mis vajab lahendust,mure
🇪🇪 **2.** keeruline teoreetiline, teaduslik küsimus, millel seni puudub ammendav vastus
🇬🇧 **1.** problem, something hindering, a worrying fact or situation that needs a solution and needs to be dealt with, a difficult, difficult thing, a complex question that needs a solution, concern
🇬🇧 **2.** a complex theoretical, scientific question that has no exhaustive answer so far

**Näited/Examples:**
● Tehnilised probleemid. ||Technical problems.||
● Tal on suhtlemisega probleeme. ||He has problems with communication.||
● Naistearsti vahetada pole mingi probleem. ||Changing a gynecologist is no problem.||
● Keskkonnaprobleemid. ||Environmental problems.||
● Terviseprobleemid. ||Health problems.||
● Mul tekkisid töö juures probleemid. ||I had problems at work.||
● Teadvus kui teaduslik probleem. ||Consciousness as a scientific problem.||
