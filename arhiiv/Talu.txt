**Tänane WOTD**
Talu - Nimisõna/Noun
*talu, talu, talu; talud, talude, talusid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** maal asuv hrl ühele perekonnale kuuluv elupaik ja majapidamine, mis hõlmab nii maavalduse kui hooned,maal ühele perele kuuluvad hooned ja maa, kus peetakse loomi ja haritakse põldu. Mõnikord tegutseb talu ka ettevõttena.
🇪🇪 **2.** (taluhoonete kohta)
🇬🇧 **1.** farm, place in the countryside a residence and household belonging to one family, which includes both land and buildings, in the countryside buildings and land owned by one family, where animals are kept and fields are cultivated. Sometimes the farm also operates as a business.
🇬🇧 **2.** (about farm buildings)

**Näited/Examples:**
● Suviti puhkame esivanemate talus. ||In the summer, we rest on our ancestors' farm.||
● Omal ajal olid Mulgimaal jõukad talud. ||At one time there were prosperous farms in Mulgimaa.||
● Müügil on Kadarbiku talu smuutid. ||Kadarbiku farm smoothies are for sale.||
● Selles talus kasvatatakse kartulit ja teravilja. ||Potatoes and cereals are grown on this farm.||
● Peame talus kanu ja lambaid. ||We have chickens and sheep on the farm.||
● Talu põles maha. ||The farm burned down.||
