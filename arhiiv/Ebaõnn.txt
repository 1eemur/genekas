**Tänane WOTD**
Ebaõnn - Nimisõna/Noun
*ebaõnn, ebaõnne, ebaõnne; ebaõnned, ebaõnnede, ebaõnni/ebaõnnesid*
*Lühike Sisseütlev: ebaõnne*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 millegi luhtumine, edu puudumine 
🇬🇧 misfortune, bad luck, the failure of something, a lack of success

**Näited/Examples:**
● Sa oled ise oma ebaõnne arhitekt. ||You are the architect of your own unhappiness.||
● Jantsil oli naistega õnne ja ebaõnne. ||Jants had luck and bad luck with women.||
● Tööotsingutel tabas teda ebaõnn. ||In his job search, he was struck by bad luck.||
● Hüvasti jättes ta samas kartis et Susan'i ebaõnn jätkub. ||As she said goodbye, she worried that Susan's bad luck would continue.||
● Meil on meeskonnana ebaõnne olnud, ja ka oma eraelus. ||We've been unlucky as a team, and also in our personal lives.||
