**Tänane WOTD**
Popp - Omadussõna/Adjective
*popp, popi, poppi; popid, poppide, poppe/poppisid*
*Lühike Sisseütlev: poppi*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 moekas, eriti soositud, (väga) populaarne  
🇬🇧 trendy, especially favored, (very) popular 

**Näited/Examples:**
● Ta on Hiinas väga popp. ||He is very popular in China.||
● Ta on klassis vist kõige popim. ||She's probably the most popular in the class.||
● Piletite saamine popile filmile ei ole lihtne. ||Getting tickets to a popular movie is not easy.||
● Korstnapühkija amet pole kuigi popp. ||The chimney sweeper's profession is not very popular.||
● Füüsilise töö tegemine ei ole hetkel popp. ||Doing physical work is not popular at the moment.||
