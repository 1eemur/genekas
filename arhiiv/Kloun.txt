**Tänane WOTD**
Kloun - Nimisõna/Noun
*kloun, klouni, klouni; klounid, klounide, kloune/klounisid*
*Lühike Sisseütlev: klouni*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** mitmesuguste naljanumbritega esinev tsirkusekoomik  
🇪🇪 **2.** veiderdav, tembutav inimene   
🇬🇧 **1.** clown, a circus comedian with a variety of comedy acts
🇬🇧 **2.** clown, a clowning, pranky person 

**Näited/Examples:**
● Klouni töö on raske töö. ||Clown work is hard work.||
● Peol lõbustasid lapsi klounid Piip ja Tuut. ||Children were entertained at the party by clowns Piip and Tuut.||
● Oleme väikesed klounid ja armastame muusika saatel tantsida. ||We are little clowns and we love to dance to music.||
● Miks teda klouniks peeti? ||Why was he considered a clown?||
● Ta on eluaeg paras kloun olnud, ega teda saa tõsiselt võtta. ||He's been a clown all his life, and can't be taken seriously.||
● Eesti rahvas ei taha klouni mängida ega end lolliks teha. ||The people of Estonia don't want to play the clown or make fools of themselves.||
