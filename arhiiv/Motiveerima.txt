**Tänane WOTD**
Motiveerima - Tegusõna/Verb
*motiveerima, motiveeridas; motiveerin, motiveerisin; motiveerinud, motiveeritud*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** kedagi midagi (innustunult) tegema panema, hrl millegagi tema tahet mõjutades  
🇪🇪 **2.** millelegi põhjendust, selgitusi esitama, midagi millegagi seletama või õigustama  
🇬🇧 **1.** to motivate, to make somebody do something (enthusiastically), usually by doing something to influence his will
🇬🇧 **2.** to give reasons, explanations, or justifications for something

**Näited/Examples:**
● Mis motiveerib sponsoreid? ||What motivates sponsors?||
● Mind motiveerib õppima teadmishimu. ||I am motivated to learn by curiosity.||
● Milline muusika motiveerib ja inspireerib sind? ||What kind of music motivates and inspires you?||
● Väga raske on motiveerida noori Pärnusse tulema. ||It is very difficult to motivate young people to come to Pärnu.||
● Kohus avaldas motiveeritud otsuse. ||The Court published a reasoned decision.||
