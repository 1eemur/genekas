Imeline - Omadussõna/Adjective
*imeline, imelise, imelist; imelised, imeliste, imelisi*
*Lühike Sisseütlev: imelisse*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 imena tunduv, imestama panev; seletamatult hea, meeldiv tunduv 
🇬🇧 miraculous, seeming wondrous, astonishing; seeming inexplicably good, pleasant

**Näited/Examples:**
● Ta on imeline inimene! ||She is a wonderful person!||
● Imeline jõulumälestus. ||A wonderful Christmas memory.||
● Reisist on mulle jäänud hulk imelisi mälestusi. ||The trip has left me with a lot of wonderful memories.||
● Saarestiku imeline loodus ei jäta kedagi külmaks. ||The wonderful nature of the archipelago leaves no one indifferent.||
● Linn on eelkõige tuntud oma imelise arhitektuuri ja kauni vanalinna poolest. ||The city is best known for its wonderful architecture and beautiful old town.||
