**Tänane WOTD**
Esiklaps - Nimisõna/Noun
*esiklaps, esiklapse, esiklast; esiklapsed, esiklaste, esiklapsi*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** (perre) esimesena sündinud laps
🇪🇪 **2.** (kellegi esimese teose vms kohta)
🇬🇧 **1.** (of a family) the first-born child
🇬🇧 **2.** (about someone's first work, etc.)

**Näited/Examples:**
● Pere esiklaps nägi ilmavalgust eelmise aasta juunis. ||The firstborn child in the family was born in June last year.||
● Näitlejapaari esiklaps peaks sündima suve algul. ||The first child of the acting couple should be born at the beginning of summer.||
● Olin esiklaps ja sageli üksi, kuna elasime ainult kolmekesi. ||I was the firstborn child and often alone, as there were only three of us living together.||
● Pere esiklaps õppis lenduriks, see on suhteliselt harv elukutsevalik. ||The firstborn child in the family studied to become a pilot, which is a relatively rare career choice.||
● Esitletav autoriplaat on muusiku esiklaps. ||The presented author's album is the musician's first work.||
