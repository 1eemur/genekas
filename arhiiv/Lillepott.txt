**Tänane WOTD**
Lillepott - Nimisõna/Noun
*lillepott, lillepoti, lillepotti; lillepotid, lillepottide, lillepotte/lillepottisid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** anum, milles kasvatatakse peamiselt lilli,nõu, mille sees saab taimi kasvatada
🇬🇧 **1.** flower pot, a container that mainly grows flowers, dishes that plants can be grown in

**Näited/Examples:**
● Täitsin lillepoti mullaga. ||I filled the flowerpot with soil.||
● Raimo kasvatas lillepottides kanepit. ||Raimo grew cannabis in flower pots.||
● Aknalaual on lillepotid kaktustega. ||There are flowerpots with cacti on the windowsill.||
● Õitsev lillepott laual loob õdusa tunde. ||The flowering flower pot on the table creates a cozy feeling.||
● Kevad on käes, linnaväljakut ehivad taas lillepotid. ||Spring is here, the city square is again decorated with flower pots.||
