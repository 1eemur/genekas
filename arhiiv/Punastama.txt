Punastama - Tegusõna/Verb
*Punastama, Punastada; Punastab, Punastas; Punastanud, Punastatud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** näost äkki punaseks minema (nt häbist, piinlikkusest, heameelest)
🇬🇧 **1.** to suddenly turn red in the face (e.g. from shame, embarrassment, pleasure)

**Näited/Examples:**
● Vahel lausa punastan piinlikkusest. ||Sometimes I downright blush with embarrassment.||
● Seksist rääkimine on jänkisid alati punastama pannud. ||Talking about sex has always made yankees blush.||
● Ta vaatas mulle silma, kohmetus ja punastas kergelt. ||He looked me in the eye, awkward and blushed slightly.||
● Laura punastas komplimendi tõttu ning naeratas poisile. ||Laura blushed at the compliment and smiled at the boy.||
● See oli niivõrd ootamatu, et ma punastasin juuksejuurteni. ||It was so unexpected that I blushed to the roots of my hair.||

**Sünonüümid/Synonyms:** 
(harv) punastuma
