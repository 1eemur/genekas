**Tänane WOTD**
Annetus - Nimisõna/Noun
*annetus, annetuse, annetust; annetused, annetuste, annetusi*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** heategevuse otstarbel heast tahtest antud rahasumma, ese vms,rahasumma või ese, mille sa annad kellegi või millegi heaks, aitamiseks
🇬🇧 **1.** donation, money for the purpose of charity, a sum of money, an object, etc., given out of good will, a sum of money or an object that you give to someone or something

**Näited/Examples:**
● Toiduannetus. ||Food donation.||
● Koguti annetusi uue haigla ehitamiseks. ||Donations were collected to build a new hospital.||
● Kogutud annetustega toetati naiste varjupaiku. ||The donations collected were used to support women's shelters.||
● Kõik rahalised toetused ja annetused kantakse sihtasutuse arvele. ||All financial support and donations are transferred to the foundation's account.||
● Kontserdil on võimalik teha annetusi kodu kaotanud laste heaks. ||At the concert, it is possible to make donations for children who have lost their homes.||
