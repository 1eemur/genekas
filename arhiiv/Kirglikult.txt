**Tänane WOTD**
Kirglikult - Määrsõna/Adverb
*kirglikult*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 tugevatest tunnetest, tundmustest lähtudes; selliseid tundeid omades, väga innukalt  
🇬🇧 passionately, on the basis of strong emotions, feelings; having such feelings, very enthusiastically

**Näited/Examples:**
● Sa võitled kirglikult. ||You fight with passion.||
● Tantsite kirglikult. ||You dance with passion.||
● Ta teeb oma tööd nii kirglikult. ||He does his work so passionately.||
● Ta armastas meest kirglikult ja eeldas, et meeski armastab. ||She loved the man passionately and assumed that the man loved her.||
● Ma eelistan elada kirglikult ja see on ohtlik, sest me ei tea ju iialgi, mis meid ees ootab. ||I prefer to live life passionately, which is dangerous because we never know what lies ahead.||
