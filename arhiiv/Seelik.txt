**Tänane WOTD**
Seelik - Nimisõna/Noun
*seelik, seeliku, seelikut; seelikud, seelikute, seelikuid*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 naiste riideese, mis ulatub vöökohalt allapoole, katab keha alumise osa ja osaliselt ka jalad  
🇬🇧 skirt, an article of clothing, usually worn by women and girls, that hangs from the waist and covers the lower part of the body

**Näited/Examples:**
● Siidseelik. ||Silk skirt.||
● Lühike seelik. ||Short skirt.||
● See seelik ei sobi sulle. ||This skirt doesn't suit you.||
● Tal olid seljas sviiter ja seelik. ||She had on a sweater and a skirt.||
● Tüdruk silub käega seelikut. ||The girl smooths her skirt with her hand.||
