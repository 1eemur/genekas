Tahtlikult - Määrsõna/Adverb
*tahtlikult*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 N/A
🇬🇧 by choice, deliberately, intentionally

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kui sa saboteerid meie reisi tahtlikult, saad sa sellega hästi hakkama. ||If you're deliberately trying to sabotage the trip, you're doing a pretty good job.||
● Ta ei varja midagi tahtlikult.||He's not hiding anything by choice.||
● Ta tegi seda tahtlikult. ||He did it deliberately.||
● Lennukiõnnetuse põhjustas tahtlikult lennuki kaaspiloot, Andreas Lubitz. ||The crash was intentionally caused by the co-pilot, Andreas Lubitz.||
