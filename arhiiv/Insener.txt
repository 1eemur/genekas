Insener - Nimisõna/Noun
*insener, inseneri, inseneri; insenerid, inseneride, insenere*
*Lühike Sisseütlev: inseneri*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 mingi tehnikaharu spetsialist, hrl tehniliste lahenduste, tehnosüsteemide, seadmete ja toodete konstrueerija, looja ja uuendaja 
🇬🇧 engineer, specialist in a branch of technology, designer, creator and innovator of technical solutions, technical systems, equipment and products

**Näited/Examples:**
● Robootika insener. ||A robotics engineer.||
● Mida teeb insener? ||What does an engineer do?||
● Olen sõjaväe insener, nagu ma ütlesin. ||I'm a military engineer, like I said.||
● Ma ei teadnud, et ta on mereväe insener. ||I didn't know he was a naval engineer.||
● Ta on insener ja tegeleb uut tüüpi dialüüsiaparaadi väljatöötamisega. ||He’s an engineer and he works at developing a new kind of dialysis machine.||
