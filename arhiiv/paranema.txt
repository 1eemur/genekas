
**Tänane WOTD**
Paranema - Tegusõna/Verb
*paranema, paraneda; paraneb, paranes; paranenud, paranetud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** paremaks muutuma
🇪🇪 **2.** heasse tervislikku seisundisse jõudma, tervist korda saama, enam mitte haige olema,enam mitte haige olema, hea tervise juurde jõudma
🇬🇧 **1.** to get better
🇬🇧 **2.** to get in a good state of health, to get healthy, to be no longer sick, to be no longer sick, to be in good health

**Näited/Examples:**
● Mitu ettevõtjat on mõista andnud, et olukord ei parane enne, kui vallandub hinnasõda. ||Several entrepreneurs have made it clear that the situation will not improve until a price war breaks out.||
● Naabrite suhted on paranenud. ||Relations between neighbors have improved.||
● Olukord paranes tunduvalt. ||The situation improved considerably.||
● Haige paranes kiiresti. ||The patient recovered quickly.||
● Ravi kestab, kuni jalg paraneb. ||Treatment continues until the leg heals.||
● Haav ei taha paraneda. ||The wound does not want to heal.||
● Laps pole veel gripist täielikult paranenud. ||The child has not yet fully recovered from the flu.||
