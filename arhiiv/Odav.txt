**Tänane WOTD**
Odav - Omadussõna/Adjective
*odav, odava, odavat; odavad, odavate, odavaid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** vähe raha maksev, madala hinnaga,selline, mis maksab vähe
🇪🇪 **2.** väikesi kulusid, väljaminekuid nõudev
🇪🇪 **3.** millegi poolest madalalt hinnatud, sellisena tühine,labasusse kalduv
🇬🇧 **1.** Little money paying, at a low price, one that pays little
🇬🇧 **2.** with small expenses, not requiring expenditure
🇬🇧 **3.** in some way low-valued, as trivial, leaning on

**Näited/Examples:**
● Ostsin odava auto. ||I bought a cheap car.||
● Elekter on praegu suhteliselt odav. ||Electricity is currently relatively inexpensive.||
● Selles poes on väga odavad kaubad. ||There are very cheap goods in this store.||
● Eelistan odavale hinnale kvaliteeti. ||I prefer quality to a cheap price.||
● Hobuse pidamine ei ole odav lõbu. ||Keeping a horse is not cheap fun.||
● Odav tööjõud. ||Cheap labor.||
● Odav nali. ||A cheap joke.||
