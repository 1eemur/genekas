**Tänane WOTD**
Mannerg - Nimisõna/Noun
*mannerg, mannergu, mannergut; mannergud, mannergute, mannerguid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** (väiksem) sangaga plekknõu hrl toiduainete jaoks
🇬🇧 **1.** (smaller) pail, container with a handle, usually for foodstuffs

**Näited/Examples:**
● Piimamannerg. ||Milk pail.||
● Kilumannerg. ||Pail of sprats.||
● Ainult piima mannergute kolinat oli kuulda. ||Only the rattle of the milk pail could be heard.||
● Hetkel pole aega mannerguga tegeleda. ||At the moment, there's no time to deal with the pail.||
● Lusikaga võtad mannergu pealt koore. ||With a spoon, you take the cream from the top of the mannergu.||
