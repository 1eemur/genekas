**Tänane WOTD**
Kruvi - Nimisõna/Noun
*kruvi, kruvi, kruvi; kruvid, kruvide, kruve/kruvisid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** materjalisse keeratav piklik peaga kinnitusvahend
🇪🇪 **2.** (laeva sõukruvi kohta)
🇬🇧 **1.** screw, an oblong head fastener screwed into the material
🇬🇧 **2.** (for a ship's propeller)

**Näited/Examples:**
● Ristpeaga kruvi. ||Phillips head screw.||
● Alustada tuli roostetanud kruvide lahtikeeramisega. ||We had to start by unscrewing the rusted screws.||
● Lauad on kinnitatud pikkade kruvidega. ||The boards are attached with long screws.||
● Naelte ja kruvide jaoks on vaja augud ette puurida. ||Holes need to be pre-drilled for the pins and screws.||
● Harilaiu madalikul kaotas laev tüüri ja vigastas kruvi. ||At Harilaiu shoal, the ship lost its rudder and damaged the propeller.||
