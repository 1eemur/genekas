Temavanune - Omadussõna/Adjective
*temavanune, temavanuse, temavanust; temavanused, temavanuste, temavanuseid*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 temaga võrreldes sama vana olev 
🇬🇧 their age, being the same age as them

**Näited/Examples:**
● Mõlemad olid temavanused, ehk umbes neljakümneviiesed. ||Both were his age, or about forty-five.||
● Kui mina olin temavanune, toetasin ma naist, last. ||When I was his age, I supported a wife and kid.||
● Temavanune poiss peaks koos sõpradega õues tegutsema. ||A boy his age needs to be outside with his friends.||
