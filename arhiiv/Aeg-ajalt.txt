**Tänane WOTD**
Aeg-ajalt - Määrsõna/Adverb
*aeg-ajalt*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 mõne aja tagant kordudes, mõnel juhul ette tulles  
🇬🇧 from time to time, recurring at intervals, in some cases occurring in advance 

**Näited/Examples:**
● Külalisi oli aeg-ajalt. ||There were visitors every now and then.||
● Tunnen end aeg-ajalt lihtsalt üksikuna. ||I just feel lonely sometimes.||
● Ta hakkab vanaks jääma ja vajab aeg-ajalt abi. ||He is getting old and needs help from time to time.||
● Aeg-ajalt kirjutab ta ka kolumne Ilta-Sanomatesse. ||From time to time she also writes a column for Ilta-Sanomat.||
● Aeg-ajalt kipun ikka negatiivselt mõtlema, kuid nüüd ma oskan seda vaos hoida. ||I still tend to think negatively from time to time, but now I know how to keep it at bay.||
 
**Sünonüümid/Synonyms:** 
mõnikord, teinekord, vahel, vahetevahel, vahest
