Survestama - Tegusõna/Verb
*survestama, survestada; survestab, survestas; survestanud, survestatud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** kedagi tugevalt mõjutama sunnimeetodite, ähvarduste abil vm viisil, kellelegi survet avaldama
🇪🇪 **2.** rõhu all kokku surutud olekusse, surve alla panema
🇬🇧 **1.** to strongly influence someone using coercive methods, threats, etc., to put pressure on someone
🇬🇧 **2.** to compress under pressure, to put under pressure

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kaablis oli survestatud lämmastik. ||The cable contained pressurized nitrogen.||
● Venemaa survestas Gruusiat kaupade sisseveo keeluga. ||Russia pressured Georgia with a ban on the import of goods.||
● Suve jooksul survestatakse kontrolliks kõik linna soojustorud. ||During the summer, all heat pipes in the city are pressurized for inspection.||
● Reklaamis kasutatavad võtted survestavad isikuid aparaati ostma. ||The techniques used in advertising put pressure on people to buy the device.||
● Majanduskasvu aeglustumine võib survestada keskpanka intressi alandama. ||The slowdown in economic growth may pressure the central bank to lower interest rates.||
