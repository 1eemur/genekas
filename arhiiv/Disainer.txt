**Tänane WOTD**
Disainer - Nimisõna/Noun
*disainer, disaineri, disainerit; disainerid, disainerite, disainereid*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 tarbeesemete, rõivaste, veebikeskkonna vm toodete ja teenuste loov arendaja ja kujundaja 
🇬🇧 designer, creative developer and designer of products and services for consumer goods, apparel, online environment, etc.

**Näited/Examples:**
● Tootedisainer. ||Product designer.||
● Kajastused meedias aitavad leida disaineritel kliente. ||Media coverage helps designers find clients.||
● Näitusel esitleti noorte disainerite töid. ||The works of young designers were presented at the exhibition.||
● Tallinnas saab uudistada Islandi disainerite ja arhitektide paremaid töid. ||In Tallinn, you can explore the best work of Icelandic designers and architects.||
● Messist võtavad osa maailma juhtivad mööblitootjad, disainerid ja hankijad. ||The world's leading furniture manufacturers, designers, and suppliers will take part in the fair.||
