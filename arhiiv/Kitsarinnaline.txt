Kitsarinnaline - Omadussõna/Adjective
*kitsarinnaline, kitsarinnalise, kitsarinnalist; kitsarinnalised, kitsarinnaliste, kitsarinnalisi*
*Lühike Sisseütlev: kitsarinnalisse*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 piiratud silmaringiga, piiratud arusaamadega; piiratud arusaamadest tingitud, väiklane 
🇬🇧 narrow-minded, with limited horizons, with limited perceptions, limited by perceptions, petty

**Näited/Examples:**
● Ma armastan sind, kuigi sa oled mõnikord kitsarinnaline. ||I love you, even if you are sometimes narrow-minded.||
● Te ei ole kitsarinnaline. ||You're not narrow-minded.||
● Sa oled nii kitsarinnaline, kas sa tead seda? ||You are so narrow-minded, you know that?||
● Yivo õpetas mulle, milline kitsarinnaline preili ma olin. ||Yivo has taught me what a narrow-minded prude I was before.||
● Jumal, see oli minust kitsarinnaline. ||God, that was petty of me.||
