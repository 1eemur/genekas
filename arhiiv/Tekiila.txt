**Tänane WOTD**
Tekiila - Nimisõna/Noun
*tekiila, tekiila, tekiilat; tekiilad, tekiilade, tekiilasid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** Mehhikost pärit kange alkohoolne jook, mida saadakse sinise agaavi mahla kääritades
🇬🇧 **1.** tequila, strong alcoholic beverage from Mexico obtained by fermenting blue agave juice

**Näited/Examples:**
● Tekiila ja snäkid olid laual! ||Tequila and snacks were on the table!||
● Parim tekiila on tehtud puhtast sinisest agaavist. ||The best tequila is made from pure blue agave.||
● Tekiila valmistamisel on olulisem osa laagerdumisel. ||The most important part of making tequila is the aging process.||
● Alguses võtsin mõned pitsid tekiilat ja siis läksin tantsima. ||At first, I took a few shots of tequila and then I went to dance.||
● Tekiila tootmine on Mehhikos range riikliku kontrolli all. ||The production of tequila is under strict national control in Mexico.||
