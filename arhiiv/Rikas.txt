**Tänane WOTD**
Rikas - Omadussõna/Adjective
*rikas, rikka, rikkat; rikkad, rikkate, rikkaid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** rohkesti raha ja varandust omav,selline, kellel on palju raha ja vara
🇪🇪 **2.** inimene, kellel on palju raha vm varandust,inimene, kellel on palju raha või vara
🇪🇪 **3.** midagi (head) rohkesti sisaldav,mitut laadi, mitmes mõttes mitmekesine,selline, kus on mingit (head) asja palju
🇬🇧 **1.** having a lot of money and wealth, one who has a lot of money and property
🇬🇧 **2.** a person who has a lot of money or wealth, a person who has a lot of money or property
🇬🇧 **3.** containing something (good) in abundance, of many kinds, diverse in many ways, the kind where there is a lot of some (good) thing

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kõik tahavad rikkaks saada. ||Everyone wants to get rich.||
● Rikastes riikides on pension 60 protsenti palgast. ||In rich countries, the pension is 60 percent of the salary.||
● Tema isa on väga rikas ärimees. ||His father is a very rich businessman.||
● Vaeste ja rikaste vaheline lõhe süveneb. ||The gap between the rich and the poor is widening.||
● Nad elavad rikaste linnaosas. ||They live in a rich neighborhood.||
● Tal on rikas fantaasia. ||He has a rich imagination.||
● Maavarade poolest rikas maa. ||A land rich in mineral resources.||
● Rikka sõnavara ja nõtke stiiliga esseist. ||An essayist with a rich vocabulary and a graceful style.||
● See piirkond on rikas järvede poolest. ||This region is rich in lakes.||
