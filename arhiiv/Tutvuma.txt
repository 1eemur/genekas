**Tänane WOTD**
Tutvuma - Tegusõna/Verb
*tutvuma, tutvuda; tutvun, tutvusin; tutvunud, tutvutud*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** (inimeste kohta:) suhtlemisel üksteisest teada saama, kedagi tuttavaks pidama hakkama 
🇪🇪 **1.1** vahetul kokkupuutel millegi tundmatu kohta teadmisi omandama 
🇬🇧 **1.** (about people) to get to know each other when communicating, to get to know somebody 
🇬🇧 **1.1** to acquire knowledge about something unknown through direct contact with it 

**Näited/Examples:**
● Tutvusime reisil. ||We met on a trip.||
● Meeldiv tutvuda. ||Nice to meet you.||
● Rõõm on teiega tutvuda. ||It's a pleasure to meet you.||
● Turistid läksid ööeluga tutvuma. ||The tourists went to familiarize themselves with the nightlife.||
● Komisjon sõitis kohapeale olukorraga tutvuma. ||The commission travelled to the site to familiarize itself with the situation.||
