**Tänane WOTD**
Alla laadima - Tegusõna/Verb
*alla laadima, alla laadida; laadin alla, laadisin alla; alla laadinud, alla laaditud*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 andmeid, faile vms internetist, serverist vm oma arvutisse võtma  
🇬🇧 to download, to retrieve data, files, etc. from the internet, a server, etc. to your computer

**Näited/Examples:**
● Taotluse vormi saad alla laadida SIIT. ||The application form can be downloaded HERE.||
● Seda on juba 10 000 korda alla laetud. ||It has already been downloaded 10,000 times.||
● Mõningaid neist on võimalik alla laadida madala resolutsiooniga failidena. ||Some of them can be downloaded as low resolution files.||
● Ma kuulan igat kurba lugu, mida olen alla laadinud, korduvalt. ||I'm going to listen to every sad song I've ever downloaded, on repeat.||
● Internetist saab alla laadida uusi helinaid, ikoone ning animatsioone. ||New sounds, icons and animations can be downloaded from the internet.||
 
**Sünonüümid/Synonyms:** 
maha laadima, tõmbama
