Marineerima - Tegusõna/Verb
*Marineerima, Marineerida; Marineerib, Marineeris; Marineerinud, Marineeritud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** (liha, köögivilja, seente vms kohta:) hapet, õli ja mitmesuguseid maitseaineid sisaldavas segus hoidma (maitsestamiseks, pehmendamiseks) või hoidistama
🇪🇪 **2.** (kõnekeelne) midagi (sihilikult) enda valduses kinni pidama, seista laskma
🇬🇧 **1.** (for meat, vegetables, mushrooms, etc.) to marinate, to keep in a mixture containing acid, oil and various seasonings (for flavoring, softening) or to store
🇬🇧 **2.** (colloquial) to keep something (intentionally) in one's possession, to let stand

**Näited/Examples:**
● Marineeritud angerjas. ||Marinated eel.||
● Õllega marineeritud praad. ||Steak marinated in beer.||
● Laske lihal läbi öö marineerida. ||Let the meat marinate overnight.||
● Aitasin emal kurke marineerida. ||I helped my mother pickle cucumbers.||
● Telekokk marineeris kala sidrunimahlaga. ||The TV chef marinated the fish in lemon juice.||
