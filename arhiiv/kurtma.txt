
**Tänane WOTD**
Kurtma - Tegusõna/Verb
*kurtma, kurta; kurdab, kurtis; kurtnud, kurdetud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** kellelegi millestki halvast, ebameeldivast, muret tekitavast või häirivast asjaolust muretsedes rääkima,oma murest või hädast kellelegi rääkima
🇬🇧 **1.** to tell someone about something bad, unpleasant, worrisome or disturbing while worrying, to tell someone about your worry or trouble

**Näited/Examples:**
● Ema kurtis valu labajalas. ||The mother complained of pain in her foot.||
● Tudeng kurtis kõrge üürihinna üle. ||The student complained about the high rent.||
● Tal on väga raske elu, aga ta ei kurda kunagi. ||He has a very hard life, but he never complains.||
● Haige kurdab peavalu. ||The patient complains of a headache.||
● Mees kurdab oma tervise üle. ||A man complains about his health.||
● Naine kurdab, et tal on vähe raha. ||The woman complains that she has little money.||
