**Tänane WOTD**
Äkki - Määrsõna/Adverb
*äkki*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** üllatuslikult (kiiresti, sel samal hetkel), ette nägematult, ette teatamata vms  
🇪🇪 **2.** osutab oletatavale või loodetavale võimalusele, milles siiski ei olda päris kindel  
🇬🇧 **1.** suddenly, unexpectedly (quickly, at that very moment), unforeseen, unannounced, etc. 
🇬🇧 **2.** maybe, refers to a hypothetical or hoped-for possibility that is not entirely certain

**Näited/Examples:**
● Äkki läks pimedaks. ||Suddenly it went dark.||
● Nüüd äkki taipas ta, mis teoksil on. ||Now he suddenly realized what was happening.||
● Äkki on ta vaim. ||Maybe she's a ghost.||
● Äkki reageerin üle. ||Maybe I'm overreacting.||
● Äkki sa oled minust kuulnud. ||Maybe you've heard of me.||
● Äkki prooviks keegi teda asendada? ||Maybe someone could try to replace him?||
