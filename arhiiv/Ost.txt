**Tänane WOTD**
Ost - Nimisõna/Noun
*ost, ostu, ostu; ostud, ostude, oste/ostusid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** millegi soetamine, omandamine raha eest,millegi ostmine
🇪🇪 **2.** see, mis on ostetud,see, mille sa ostnud oled
🇬🇧 **1.** purchase of something, acquiring money, buying something
🇬🇧 **2.** that what is purchased, the one you have bought

**Näited/Examples:**
● Kogume uue korteri ostuks raha. ||We are raising money to buy a new apartment.||
● Kodus uurisin oma oste lähemalt. ||At home, I studied my purchases in more detail.||
● Praegu pole aktsiate ostuks kõige parem aeg. ||Now is not the best time to buy shares.||
● Internet pakub võimaluse teha oste kodust lahkumata. ||The Internet offers the opportunity to make purchases without leaving home.||
● Kui te pole ostuga rahul, saate raha tagasi. ||If you are not happy with your purchase, you will get the money back.||
● Minu viimane suur ost oli auto. ||My last big purchase was a car.||
