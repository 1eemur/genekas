Tikksirge - Omadussõna/Adjective
*tikksirge, tikksirge, tikksirget; tikksirged, tikksirgete, tikksirgeid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** väga, täiesti sirge
🇬🇧 **1.** very, totally straight

**Näited/Examples:**
● Tikksirged männid. ||Straight pines.||
● Naine istub nüüd tikksirge seljaga. ||The woman now sits with a straight back.||
● Selg pidi olema tikksirge jne. ||The back was supposed to be totally straight, etc.||
● Poiss tüürib tikksirge seljaga tantsupõrandale. ||The boy steers with a straight back onto the dance floor.||
● Jordaania piirilt Bagdadi viib tikksirge ja sile asfalttee, mida ääristab ainult liiv. ||From the Jordanian border to Baghdad, there's a straight and smooth asphalt road, lined only by sand.||
