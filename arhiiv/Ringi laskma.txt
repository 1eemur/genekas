**Tänane WOTD**
Ringi laskma - Tegusõna/Verb
*ringi laskma, ringi lasta; lasen ringi, lasksin ringi/lasin ringi; ringi lastud, ringi lasknud*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** kergemeelseid armusuhteid harrastama 
🇪🇪 **2.** ringi liikuma, ringi hulkuma 
🇬🇧 **1.** to fool around, to engage in frivolous love affairs 
🇬🇧 **2.** to move around, to wander around 

**Näited/Examples:**
● Ma mõtlesin, et sa tahad Ericu õega ringi lasta. ||I thought you wanted to fool around with Eric's sister.||
● Tom, mul ei ole aega, et niisama ringi lasta. ||Tom, I don't have time to fool around.||
● Hobune armastab muru peal ringi lasta. ||The horse loves to run around on the grass.||
● Miks peaks mees revolvriga ringi käima ja laskma end solvata? ||Why should a man walk around with a pistol and then let himself be insulted?||
● Doktor, ma ei saa lasta tal ringi jalutada. ||I can't have him walking around, Doc.||
