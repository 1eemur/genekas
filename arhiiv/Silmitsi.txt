**Tänane WOTD**
Silmitsi - Määrsõna/Adverb
*silmitsi*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 (vahetult, lähedalt) vastakuti, (raskustega) vastamisi  
🇬🇧 (directly, intimately) facing each other, (with difficulty) face to face, up against something, confronting

**Näited/Examples:**
● Alati silmitsi ebaõnne naeratades ja küüniline märkus. ||Always facing adversity with a smile and a cynical remark.||
● Me olime silmitsi kahe tähtsa probleemiga. ||We were faced with two important problems.||
● Nad seisavad silmitsi kuue relvastatud mehega. ||They are confronted by six armed men.||
● Esimest korda silmitsi surmaga. ||Facing death for the first time.||

**Sünonüümid/Synonyms:** 
silmsi
