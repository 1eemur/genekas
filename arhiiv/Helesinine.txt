Helesinine - Omadussõna/Adjective
*helesinine, helesinise, helesinist; helesinised, helesiniste, helesiniseid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** heledamat tooni sinine
🇬🇧 **1.** a lighter shade of blue

**Näited/Examples:**
● Helesinised silmad. ||Light blue eyes.||
● Unistus helesinisest merest. ||A dream of a bright blue sea.||
● Värvisime maja helesiniseks. ||We painted the house light blue.||
● Tüdrukul on jalas helesinised teksased. ||The girl is wearing light blue jeans.||
● Pilved hajusid ja taevas muutus helesiniseks. ||The clouds cleared and the sky turned light blue.||
