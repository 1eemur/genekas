**Tänane WOTD**
Kinkima - Tegusõna/Verb
*kinkima, kinkida; kingib, kinkis; kinkinud, kingitud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** kellelegi tähelepanuavaldusena midagi andma,kellelegi midagi vastutasuta andma,kingituseks andma
🇪🇪 **2.** mingit (hrl pahandavat, ülekohtust) asjaolu niisama jätma, seda kellelegi andestama
🇬🇧 **1.** to gift, to give something to someone as a sign of attention, to give something to someone for free, to give as a gift
🇬🇧 **2.** to leave some (especially annoying, unfair) circumstance as it is, to forgive someone for it


**Näited/Examples:**
● Sõber kinkis mulle käevõru. ||The friend gave me a bracelet.||
● Kuningas kinkis vangile vabaduse. ||The king granted the prisoner freedom.||
● Poisile kingiti sünnipäevaks jalgpall. ||The boy was given a football for his birthday.||
● Vanemad kinkisid lapsele jõuludeks suusad. ||The parents gave the child skis for Christmas.||
● Kollektsionäär kinkis oma maalikogu muuseumile. ||The collector donated his painting collection to the museum.||
