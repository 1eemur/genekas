**Tänane WOTD**
Kindlasti - Määrsõna/Adverb
*kindlasti*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 täie kindlusega, ilma et mingit kahtlust tekiks 
🇬🇧 certainly, surely, with absolute certainty, without any doubt

**Näited/Examples:**
● Te saite kindlasti valesti aru. ||You must have misunderstood.||
● See on kindlasti probleem ning seda ütlesime me ka parlamendikomisjonis. ||This certainly is a problem, and we said so in committee.||
● Mu isa oleks kindlasti nõus. ||My father would certainly agree.||
● Sellega saan ma kindlasti abiks olla. ||I can definitely help you with that.||
● See oli kindlasti imelik. ||It was definitely weird.||
