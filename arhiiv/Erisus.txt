Erisus - Nimisõna/Noun
*erisus, erisuse, erisust; erisused, erisuste, erisusi*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** erinevaks tegev omadus, iseloomulik joon vms,miski, mis teeb ühe inimese või asja teisest inimesest või asjast erinevaks, teistsuguseks,erinevaks tegev omadus
🇪🇪 **2.** teatav eriomadus või erijoon, erandlik asjaolu,eriomadus, erijoon
🇬🇧 **1.** dissimilarity, a different feature, a characteristic line or whatever, something that makes one person or thing different from another person or thing
🇬🇧 **2.** a certain special feature or a special feature, an exceptional circumstance, special characteristics, a special feature

**Näited/Examples:**
● Valitsegu arvamuste erisus! ||Let's rule the differences of opinions!||
● Kui otsime erisusi, siis ka leiame neid. ||If we look for differences, we will find them.||
● Mudelite peamine erisus seisneb sisemise mälu suuruses. ||The main distinction of the models is the size of the inner memory.||
● Räägiti erisuste arvestamisest kehalises kasvatuses. ||There was talk of the consideration of differences in physical education.||
● Õppeaasta lõpuks õpilane mõistab loodusainete omavahelisi seoseid ja erisusi. ||By the end of the school year, students understand the connections and differences between natural sciences.||
