**Tänane WOTD**
Salat - Nimisõna/Noun
*salat, salati, salatit; salatid, salatite, salateid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** köögiviljana kasvatatav mahlakas väike rohttaim,taim, mille suuri rohelisi lehti inimesed söövad
🇪🇪 **2.** salatitaime lehed toiduna
🇪🇪 **3.** hrl külm toit, mis on kokku segatud (väikesteks) tükkideks lõigatud eri toiduainetest ning hrl maitsestatud õli-, hapukoore- vm kastmega,enamasti külm toit, milles on väikesteks tükkideks lõigatud aedvili, muud toiduained ja mingi kaste
🇬🇧 **1.** lettuce,  small succulent herbaceous plant grown as a vegetable, a plant whose large green leaves are eaten by people
🇬🇧 **2.** lettuce leaves as food
🇬🇧 **3.** salad, a cold dish made from various foods cut into (small) pieces and seasoned with oil, sour cream or other sauce, usually a cold dish containing vegetables cut into small pieces, other foods and some kind of sauce


**Näited/Examples:**
● Kasvatan salatit, sibulat ja tilli. ||I grow lettuce, onions, and dill.||
● Ta kasvatab kodus aknalaual tilli, peterselli ja salatit. ||She grows dill, parsley, and lettuce on her windowsill at home.||
● Nirista kalale sidrunimahla, serveeri kohe salatil. ||Drizzle lemon juice over the fish, serve immediately on the salad.||
● Punase sibula ja tomati salat. ||Red onion and tomato salad.||
● Grillitud kitsejuustu salat peekoniga. ||Grilled goat cheese salad with bacon.||
● Porgandisalat. ||Carrot salad.||
● Tegin kurgist ja tomatist värske salati. ||I made a fresh salad of cucumber and tomato.||
