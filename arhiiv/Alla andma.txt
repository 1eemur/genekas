**Tänane WOTD**
Alla andma - Tegusõna/Verb
*alla andma, alla anda; annan alla, andsin alla; alla antud, alla andnud*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 vastupanust loobuma, end võidetuks tunnistama; kaotajaks jääma, mingi olukorraga leppima  
🇬🇧 to give up resistance, to admit one's defeat; to lose, to come to terms with a situation 

**Näited/Examples:**
● Ära veel alla anna. ||Don't give up yet.||
● Ma ei lase sul alla anda. ||I won't let you give up.||
● Me peame alla andma... ||We gotta surrender...||
● Oled nüüd valmis alla andma? ||Are you ready to give up now?||
● Miks me nii kergelt alla anname? ||Why are we giving up so easily?||

**Sünonüümid/Synonyms:** 
alistuma, alla vanduma
