**Tänane WOTD**
Reageering - Nimisõna/Noun
*reageering, reageeringu, reageeringut; reageeringud, reageeringute, reageeringuid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** käitumine, tegutsemine, meeleolu järsk muutus vms vastusena ärritajale (nt teatavale sündmusele, asjaolule)
🇬🇧 **1.** reaction, behavior, action, a sudden change in the mood or the like in response to an irritant (eg to a certain event, fact)

**Näited/Examples:**
● Ootamatult äge reageering. ||An unexpectedly acute reaction.||
● Õpetajad ei peaks kartma lapsevanema reageeringut. ||Teachers should not be afraid of a parent's reaction.||
● Vaatasin küsivalt talle otsa, ootasin reageeringut. ||I looked at him questioningly, waiting for a reaction.||
● Kõige tavalisem reageering loomadel on müra eest põgeneda. ||The most common response in animals is to flee from noise.||
● Ootasin hirmuga Niina reageeringut, aga ta hakkas õnneks naerma. ||I waited in fear for Niina's reaction, but luckily she started laughing.||
