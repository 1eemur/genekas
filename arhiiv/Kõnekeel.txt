🇬🇧 **1.** the collouqial language, in everyday communication (both in the letter and speech), the language of the official language, free from the official language, used in everyday communication, freer than official language
🇬🇧 **2.** language used in oral speech, oral language (contrary to written language), speaking language, oral language
