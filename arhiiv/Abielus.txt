**Tänane WOTD**
Abielus - Määrsõna/Adverb
*abielus*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 seisund, kus kaks inimest on ametlikus liidus ja hrl elavad koos 
🇬🇧 married, a situation where two people are in a formal union and usually live together

**Näited/Examples:**
● Olen abielus naine, John. ||I'm a married woman, John.||
● Mida?! Holki sai poja? Aga ta polnud ju abielus. ||What! Holk had a son? But he was not married.||
● Kas olete abielus või vallaline? ||Are you married or unmarried?||
● Pead end üleval nagu abielus mees. ||You're acting like a married man.||
● ”Me oleme 16 aastat õnnelikus abielus olnud,” räägib Tony. ||“We’ve been happily married for 16 years,” says Tony.||
