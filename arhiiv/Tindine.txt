**Tänane WOTD**
Tindine - Omadussõna/Adjective
*tindine, tindise, tindist; tindised, tindiste, tindiseid*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** tindiga koos, sellega määrdunud  
🇪🇪 **2.** tintjalt sügavtume  
🇬🇧 **1.** inky, with ink, smeared with ink
🇬🇧 **2.** inky, deep black

**Näited/Examples:**
● Tindised sõrmed. ||Inky fingers.||
● Raamat on heas korras, alumine serv tindine. ||The book is in good condition, the bottom edge is ink-stained.||
● Sa tulid koolist koju ja su käed olid tindised. ||You came home from school and your hands were covered in ink.||
● Mul on siiani käed tindised, aga loengust oli vähemalt kasu. ||I still have ink on my hands, but at least the lecture was useful.||
● Selle tindine koostis püsib kuni 24 tundi ning ei lähe laiali ega määri. ||Its ink composition lasts up to 24 hours and does not spread or smear.||
