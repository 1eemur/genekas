Osalema - Tegusõna/Verb
*osalema, osaleda; osaleb, osales; osalenud, osaletud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** koos teistega mingil üritusel, sündmusel olema, teistega koos midagi tegema,teistega koos midagi tegema
🇬🇧 **1.** to participate, to be at an event, to do something with others, to do something with others

**Näited/Examples:**
● Moemaja osales Helsingi moemessil. ||The fashion house participated in the Helsinki Fashion Fair.||
● Küsitluses osales 3000 inimest. ||3,000 people participated in the survey.||
● Osalesin valimistel vaatlejana. ||I participated in the elections as an observer.||
● Jah, ma olen osalenud filmide tegemisel. ||Yes, I have been involved in making films.||
● Konkursil osaleb palju õpilasi. ||Many students participate in the competition.||
● Osalen põnevas projektis. ||I am participating in an exciting project.||
