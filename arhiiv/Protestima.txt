**Tänane WOTD**
Protestima - Tegusõna/Verb
*protestima, protestida; protestin, protestisin; protestinud, protestitud*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** vastu vaidlema, mitte nõustuma; avalikult nõusolematust või vastuseisu avaldama 
🇪🇪 **1.1** millegi kohta ametlikku protesti esitama
🇬🇧 **1.** to protest, oppose, or disagree; to publicly disagree or express disagreement;
🇬🇧 **1.1** to make a formal protest about something

**Näited/Examples:**
● Laps protestib magamaminekul. ||The child protests at bedtime.||
● Ta protestis kohustusliku ajateenistuse vastu. ||He protested against compulsory military service.||
● Noored protestivad sageli oma vanemate vastu. ||Young people often protest against their parents.||
● Kohalikud elanikud protestivad selle vastu, kuidas loodusvarasid kasutatakse. ||Local people are protesting against how natural resources are being used.||
● Erahaigla protestis ravikvaliteedi nõukogu otsuse. ||A private hospital protested against a decision by the quality of care council.||
