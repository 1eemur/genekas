**Tänane WOTD**
Lollitama - Tegusõna/Verb
*Lollitama, Lollitada; Lollitab, Lollitas; Lollitanud, Lollitatud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** petma, segadusse viima, lolliks tegema
🇪🇪 **2.** tembutama, nalja tegema, lolli mängima
🇬🇧 **1.** to deceive, cheat, confuse, fool
🇬🇧 **2.** to prank, to joke, to play the fool

**Näited/Examples:**
● Ära lase suvalistel posijatel ennast lollitada! ||Don't let random soothsayers fool you!||
● Inimesi lollitatakse populistlike loosungitega. ||People are fooled by populist slogans.||
● Kogu rahvast saab lollitada natukest aega. ||You can fool the whole population for a while.||
● Tunnis kõik lollitasid. ||Everyone fooled around in class.||
● Jalgrattur sõnas, et vorm on hea ja homsele etapile ta lollitama ei lähe. ||The cyclist said that he is in good shape and that he will not be fooling around in tomorrow's stage.||
