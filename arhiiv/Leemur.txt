**Tänane WOTD**
Leemur - Nimisõna/Noun
*leemur, leemuri, leemurit; leemurid, leemurite, leemureid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** Madagaskaril ja selle lähissaartel elav suurte silmadega poolahv
🇪🇪 **2.** Rooma usundis surnu hing, kes kimbutab inimesi kurjade viirastustega
🇬🇧 **1.** lemur, a large-eyed proboscis monkey living in Madagascar and its nearby islands
🇬🇧 **2.** in Roman religion, the soul of the dead who afflicts people with evil spells

**Näited/Examples:**
● Esimest korda elus kohtusin leemuriga. ||For the first time in my life, I met a lemur.||
● Leemuri saba pikkus ületab märgatavalt keha pikkuse. ||The length of a lemur's tail is considerably longer than its body.||
● Mulle meeldisid väga mets, leemurid ja inimeste soojus. ||I really liked the forest, the lemurs and the warmth of the people.||
● Külastame leemurite parki (asub 25 km kaugusel pealinnast). ||We will visit the lemur park (which is located 25 km from the capital).||
● Esimesed elukuud veedavad pisikesed leemurid ema kasuka külge klammerdudes. ||Tiny lemurs spend their first months of life clinging to their mother's fur.||
