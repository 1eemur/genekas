**Tänane WOTD**
Vajadus - Nimisõna/Noun
*vajadus, vajaduse, vajadust; vajadused, vajaduste, vajadusi*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** millegi mittepiisavuse tajumine, mis ajendab puuduva poole püüdlema; 
🇪🇪 **2.** midagi millekski sundiv põhjus
🇬🇧 **1.** need, the perception of the inadequacy of something, which prompts to strive for what is missing
🇬🇧 **2.** a compelling reason for something

**Näited/Examples:**
● Vanemad seavad oma elu imiku vajaduste järgi. ||Parents arrange their lives according to the needs of the infant.||
● Tuleb hoolt kanda ka emotsionaalsete vajaduste rahuldamise eest. ||Care must also be taken to satisfy emotional needs.||
● Tundsin vajadust nutta. ||I felt the need to cry.||
● Suhtlemisvajadus. ||The need to communicate.||
● Lendur teatas, et tekkis vajadus maanduda. ||The pilot reported that it was necessary to land.||
