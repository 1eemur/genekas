**Tänane WOTD**
Skulptor - Nimisõna/Noun
*skulptor, skulptori, skulptorit; skulptorid, skulptorite, skulptoreid*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 kolmemõõtmelisi kunstiteoseid, kujusid tegev, raidkunsti või plastikaga tegelev kunstnik 
🇬🇧 sculptor, an artist who makes sculptures.

**Näited/Examples:**
● Skulptor vastas, et ta polnud midagi loonud. ||The sculptor replied that he hadn't created anything.||
● Tuntud prantsuse skulptor Auguste Rodin on öelnud: "Kunstnik on looduse südamesõber." ||Auguste Rodin, the famous French sculptor, said: "The artist is the confidant of nature."||
● Anna Justine Mahler (15. juuni 1904 Viin – 3. juuni 1988 Hampstead) oli Austria skulptor. ||Anna Justine Mahler (15 June 1904 – 3 June 1988) was an Austrian sculptor.||
● Kas te teate skulptor Giacomettit? ||Do you know Giacometti, the sculptor?||
