Dinosaurus - Nimisõna/Noun
*dinosaurus, dinosauruse, dinosaurust; dinosaurused, dinosauruste, dinosaurusi*
*Lühike Sisseütlev: dinosaurusse*

**Tähendused/Meanings**
🇪🇪 **1.** suur sisalikulaadne ürgaja roomaja  
🇪🇪 **2.** (väga tuntud, hrl pikka aega tegutsenud inimese kohta)  
🇬🇧 **1.** dinosaur, a fossil reptile of the Mesozoic era, in many species reaching an enormous size
🇬🇧 **2.** a dinosaur, for a very well-known person who usually has been active for a long time

**Näited/Examples:**
● Meie aias on dinosaurus. ||There's a dinosaur in our backyard.||
● Talle meeldivad dinosaurused sama palju kui sulle. ||He loves dinosaurs as much as you do.||
● Mis tunne oli elada, kui dinosaurused Maal rändasid? ||What was it like to live when the dinosaurs roamed the Earth?||
● Dinosaurused olid vapustavalt hea kohanemisvõimega ja väga head ellujääjad. ||Dinosaurs were incredibly adaptable and very good survivors.||
● Poliitiline dinosaurus. ||Political dinosaur.||
