Haruldane - Omadussõna/Adjective
*haruldane, haruldase, haruldast; haruldased, haruldaste, haruldasi*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** harva esinev, mittesage; 
🇬🇧 **1.** rare, infrequent

**Näited/Examples:**
● Siirup sisaldas vaniljet ja haruldasi taimeõlisid. ||The syrup contained vanilla and rare plant oils.||
● Maavärin on Eestis üsna haruldane nähtus. ||An earthquake is a rather rare phenomenon in Estonia.||
● Arstid ravisid geeniteraapia abil haruldase haiguse. ||Doctors treated a rare disease with gene therapy.||
● Haruldane taim eristus roheluses värvi poolest. ||The rare plant stood out in the greenery because of its color.||
● Nõiakaev meelitas haruldast nähtust vaatama suure rahvahulga. ||The witch's well attracted a large crowd to witness the rare phenomenon.||
