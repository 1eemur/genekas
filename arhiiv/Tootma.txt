**Tänane WOTD**
Tootma - Tegusõna/Verb
*tootma, toota; toodab, tootis; tootnud, toodetud*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** tootmisvahendite abil (suures koguses) asju, kaupu valmistama,midagi (suures koguses) valmistama, et seda müüa
🇪🇪 **2.** (selle tegevuse rentaabluse kohta)
🇪🇪 **3.** (üldisemalt:) midagi tekitama, looma, valmistama
🇪🇪 **4.** (organismide, elundite kohta)
🇬🇧 **1.** to make things, goods (in large quantities) with the means of production, to make something (in large quantities) in order to sell it
🇬🇧 **2.** (about the profitability of this activity)
🇬🇧 **3.** (more generally) to create, make something
🇬🇧 **4.** (about organisms, organs)

**Näited/Examples:**
● Tehas toodab arvuteid ja arvutiseadmeid. ||The factory produces computers and computer equipment.||
● Soojust tootvad ettevõtted. ||Heat producing companies.||
● See firma toodab tööriistu. ||This company produces tools.||
● Kus see auto on toodetud? ||Where is this car manufactured?||
● Firma toodab kahjumit. ||The company is making a loss.||
● Äri, mis ei suuda toota kasumit, tuleb lõpetada. ||A business that cannot make a profit must be closed.||
● Ühiskond toodab ja kinnitab stereotüüpe. ||Society produces and confirms stereotypes.||
● Organismi immuunsüsteem reageerib vaktsiinile ja hakkab tootma antikehi. ||The body's immune system reacts to the vaccine and starts producing antibodies.||
