Lumepall - Nimisõna/Noun
*lumepall, lumepalli, lumepalli; lumepallid, lumepallide, lumepalle/lumepallisid*

**Tähendused/Meanings:**
🇪🇪 **1.** sulalumest mätsitud või veeretatud pall
🇪🇪 **2.** valgete kerajate õisikutega ilupõõsas, hariliku lodjapuu aedvorm
🇪🇪 **3.** vahustatud munavalgest klimp (magustoidus)
🇬🇧 **1.** snowball, a ball stuffed or rolled from melted snow
🇬🇧 **2.** ornamental shrub with white spherical flowers, garden form of common barberry
🇬🇧 **3.** whipped egg white dumpling (in dessert)

**Näited/Examples:**
● Poisid loopisid üksteist lumepallidega. ||The boys threw snowballs at each other.||
● Skandaal paisus veereva lumepallina. ||The scandal swelled like a rolling snowball.||
● Niisket lund oli raske lumepallideks kokku suruda. ||It was hard to squeeze the wet snow into snowballs.||
● Mäest alla lükatud lumepalli pole lihtne peatada. ||It's not easy to stop a snowball pushed down a hill.||
● Serveerimisel tõsta taldrikule vanillikaste ja aseta sinna peale lumepallid. ||When serving, place the custard on a plate and place the snowballs on top.||
